Overview

守墓人从放逐信息中获取情报，石像鬼带来独特的第三方变数，信息链错综复杂，步步惊心。

The Gravedigger reads exile results for clues; the Gargoyle adds a wild third-party twist. Every step is treacherous.

✨ 神职

预言家Seer＋

每晚可以查验一名玩家的身份是好人还是狼人。Each night, check whether a player is Good or Evil.

女巫Witch＋

拥有一瓶解药和一瓶毒药。每晚至多使用一瓶药，全程不可自救。Holds one antidote and one poison. At most one per night. Cannot save herself.

猎人Hunter＋

出局时可带走一名存活玩家。被毒或梦游不可开枪。When eliminated, may take one surviving player. Cannot fire if poisoned or sleepwalking.

守墓人Gravedigger＋

从第二夜起，会得知上一个被放逐的玩家为好人还是狼人。如前日有狼人自爆，则守墓人当夜无信息。From night two, learns whether the last exiled player was Good or Evil. No info on nights following a wolf self-destruct.
