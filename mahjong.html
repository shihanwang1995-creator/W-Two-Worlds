Things to Know

麻将包间为标准4人局，请确保到场人数为4人。

The mahjong room is set up for a standard 4-player game. Please ensure exactly 4 players are present.

收费为 $30/小时，无任何附加费用，欢迎自带食品及饮料。

The rate is $30 per hour with no additional fees. Outside food and drinks are welcome.

超时30分钟以内免费；超过30分钟则按整小时计费，即额外收取 $30。

Up to 30 minutes of overtime is free; beyond 30 minutes, a full additional hour ($30) is charged.

Players agree on the ruleset among themselves. Staff do not adjudicate rule disputes — these are resolved by the players.

如需延时，请提前告知工作人员，以便安排下一场次。

If you wish to extend your session, please inform staff in advance so we can accommodate the next booking.

请爱护自动麻将桌及房间设施，如有人为损坏需照价赔偿。

Please treat the auto-shuffling table and room equipment with care. Any intentional or negligent damage will be charged accordingly.
