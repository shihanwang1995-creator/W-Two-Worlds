Synopsis

1914年（民国三年）10月23日，大海深处，新型潜艇"铁鲸号"。

昏暗的大海深处，"铁鲸号"内的诸人在经历了数日水下旅程后均感到身体不适——在失去了阳光照射的海底，似乎有什么东西在船舱外不断窥视着这群来自陆地的不速之客……

潜艇采用前置操舵台，艇内的每间船舱上方都连接着维系呼吸的"通风管道"，从"储气舱"运输空气。潜艇主人樊宗楚信誓旦旦地保证，每次上浮储满"储气舱"后，里面的空气都足够他和船长、船员、乘客在内共6人在深海潜行一周的使用。

October 23rd, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). Deep beneath the ocean — aboard the new-model submarine "Iron Whale."

In the darkness of the deep sea, the passengers of the Iron Whale have been feeling unwell after days of underwater travel. Deprived of sunlight at the ocean floor, something outside the hull seems to be watching these unwelcome visitors from the land…

The submarine is fitted with a forward helm station, and each cabin is connected to ventilation ducts that draw air from the "air reserve tank." The vessel's owner, 樊宗楚, has given his solemn assurance that after each surfacing to replenish the tank, there is enough air to sustain all six aboard — captain, crew, and passengers alike — for a full week at depth.
