Overview

催眠师每夜干扰狼队行动，瞌睡鬼以猜测代替猎杀，梦与现实的边界在此模糊，信息真假难辨。

The Hypnotist disrupts wolf actions nightly; the Sleepwalker kills through guesswork. Dream and reality blur.

✨ 神职
✨ Deity
通灵师Medium＋

每晚查验一名玩家的具体身份。Each night, learns a player's exact role identity.

女巫Witch＋

拥有一瓶解药和一瓶毒药。每晚至多使用一瓶，全程不可自救。One antidote and one poison. At most one per night. Cannot save herself.

猎人Hunter＋

出局时可带走一名存活玩家。被毒或梦游不可开枪。When eliminated, may take one surviving player. Cannot fire if poisoned or sleepwalking.

催眠师Hypnotist＋

从第二夜起，每晚选择三名玩家进入梦境。若其中有一名狼队，则当夜狼队无法击杀；若有两名狼队，则催眠师出局；三好或三狼则无事发生。每名玩家只能被催眠师选择一次。From night two, selects three players. If one is Evil — wolves cannot kill that night. If two are Evil — Hypnotist exits. Three Good or three Evil — nothing happens. Each player can only be selected by the Hypnotist once per game.

👤 Villager

🐺 狼人
🐺 Werewolf
狼人 ×3Werewolf ×3＋

每晚共同击杀一名玩家。Each night, collectively eliminate one player.

瞌睡鬼Sleepwalker＋

单独行动，与狼队互不见面，不可自爆。每晚可对一名玩家的具体身份进行猜测：猜测正确则该玩家代替狼队击杀目标成为当晚失眠者，失眠者无法被解救或守护，当夜出局。若瞌睡鬼是催眠师梦境中唯一的狼队角色，则瞌睡鬼技能永久失效。Acts alone, does not meet the wolf team, cannot self-destruct. Each night, guesses a player's exact role: if correct, that player becomes the night's insomniac and exits — immune to saves or protection. If the Sleepwalker is the only Evil in the Hypnotist's dream, their ability is permanently disabled.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

黄粱美梦为12人竞技版型，催眠师干扰狼队行动，瞌睡鬼以猜测替代猎杀，以下为完整游戏流程。

A 12-player competitive template. The Hypnotist disrupts the wolf team; the Sleepwalker kills through guesswork. Below is the complete game flow.

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共12张：通灵师、女巫、猎人、催眠师各1张，瞌睡鬼1张，狼人3张，平民4张。

The host secretly deals 12 role cards: 1 Medium, 1 Witch, 1 Hunter, 1 Hypnotist, 1 Sleepwalker, 3 Werewolves, and 4 Villagers.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

所有人闭眼。狼人睁眼互认（瞌睡鬼单独行动，不与狼队见面）。女巫查看刀口，通灵师查验一名玩家具体身份，猎人等待出局时机。从第二夜起，催眠师选择三名玩家进入梦境，瞌睡鬼猜测一名玩家的具体身份。

All close eyes. Wolves open to identify each other (Sleepwalker acts alone, does not meet wolves). Witch sees the kill; Medium checks one exact role; Hunter waits. From night two: Hypnotist selects three players; Sleepwalker guesses one player's exact role.

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参选玩家投票选出警长。警长投票权重为1.5票，出局时可传递或销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff's vote counts as 1.5 and may pass or destroy the badge upon elimination.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。猎人若被狼刀击杀，可在遗言阶段选择是否开枪带走一名存活玩家。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement. If the Hunter is killed by wolves, they may choose to take a surviving player with them.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

催眠师每晚选择三人：若其中有一名狼队，当夜狼队无法击杀；若有两名狼队，催眠师出局。瞌睡鬼猜对身份时，该玩家成为失眠者当夜出局，无法被解救。每个座位号仅能被催眠师使用一次。

Hypnotist selects three players nightly: one Evil = wolves cannot kill that night; two Evil = Hypnotist exits. If the Sleepwalker guesses correctly, the target becomes the insomniac and exits — immune to saves. Each seat number can only be selected once by the Hypnotist.

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
