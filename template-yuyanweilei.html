HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

愚言围猎为12人竞技版型，双猎人枪火交织，狼王带人出局，愚言家可吞噬狼刀令夜间循环，以下为完整游戏流程。

A 12-player competitive template. Two Hunters with overlapping fire, a Wolf King who drags someone on exit, and a Yuyan who can absorb wolf kills to force an extra night. Below is the complete game flow.

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共12张：愚言家、女巫各1张，猎人2张，狼王1张，狼人3张，平民4张。

The host secretly deals 12 role cards: 1 Yuyan, 1 Witch, 2 Hunters, 1 Wolf King, 3 Werewolves, and 4 Villagers.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

所有人闭眼。狼人与狼王睁眼互认，商定首刀目标。女巫查看刀口并决定是否使用解药。愚言家睁眼查验一名玩家身份好坏。若愚言家被选为刀口，可选择吞噬狼刀：愚言家出局，狼刀无效，跳过天亮直接进入下一个黑夜。

All close eyes. Wolves and Wolf King open to identify each other and select a kill. Witch sees the target and decides whether to save. Yuyan opens to check one player's alignment. If the Yuyan is the kill target, they may absorb the kill — the Yuyan exits, the wolf kill is nullified, and the game skips directly to the next night.

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参选玩家投票选出警长。警长投票权重为1.5票，出局时可传递或销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff's vote counts as 1.5 and may pass or destroy the badge upon elimination.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。猎人若被狼刀击杀，可在遗言阶段选择是否开枪带走一名存活玩家。若愚言家当夜吞噬狼刀，则无天亮公布，直接进入下一个黑夜。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement. If the Hunter is killed by wolves, they may take a surviving player. If the Yuyan absorbed the wolf kill, there is no dawn announcement — the game skips directly to the next night.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

所有存活玩家轮流发言并投票，得票最多的玩家被淘汰出局。双猎人出局时均可带走一名存活玩家。狼王出局时亦可带走一名玩家，被毒的猎人与狼王均不可开枪。注意：若前一夜愚言家吞噬了狼刀，则跳过本阶段，直接进入下一个黑夜。

All surviving players speak in turn and vote; the most-voted player is eliminated. Both Hunters may take a player on exit — a powerful deterrent. The Wolf King also takes a player on exit. Poisoned Hunters and Wolf King cannot fire. Note: if the Yuyan absorbed the wolf kill the previous night, this entire phase is skipped.

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
