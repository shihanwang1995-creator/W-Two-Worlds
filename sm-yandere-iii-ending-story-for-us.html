Synopsis

清晨的阳光细腻而清晰。

我走向窗边，看着寒热温差形成的水珠挂在玻璃上。它起初只是细细的一颗，缓缓地偏向了右侧，将另一颗水珠吞噬，随后陡然加速向左下方滑走。

一颗、两颗、三颗……

每一颗水珠的轨迹都是如此相似。

The morning light is soft and clear.

I walk to the window and watch the droplets clinging to the glass — born from the gap between cold air and warm. At first just one, barely there, drifting slowly to the right, absorbing another droplet, then suddenly accelerating down and to the left.

One. Two. Three…

Every droplet traces the same path.
