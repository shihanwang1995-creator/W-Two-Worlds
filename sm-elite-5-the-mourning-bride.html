Synopsis

民国三年8月3日农历六月十二，立秋前五天。上海城外西北远郊的山间，距苏州百里的"百里村"内，大地主姚长寿病卧在床，继室"红夫人"从上海请来西医调理。这一天，一场命案骤然降临，真凶就隐藏在姚家大宅之内……

The 12th day of the 6th lunar month, Year 3 of the Republic of China — five days before the Start of Autumn. Deep in the hills northwest of Shanghai, in the village of Baili, roughly a hundred li from Suzhou, the great landlord 姚长寿 lies ill in bed. His second wife, the "Red Lady," has summoned a Western doctor from Shanghai to attend to him. Then, on this very day, a murder descends upon the Yao household — and the killer is concealed within its very walls…
