天使梦魇

Angel's Nightmare

👥 12人 👥 12 Players 竞技版型 Competitive
OVERVIEW · 简介
OVERVIEW

模板简介

Overview

梦魇每夜强制梦游一名玩家令其免疫狼刀，大天使保护关键神职，剑客的后手枪随时改变局面。

Nightmare forces sleepwalking immunity nightly; the Archangel shields vital roles; the Swordsman's delayed shot can change everything.

✨ 神职
✨ Deity
预言家Seer＋

每晚查验一名玩家是好人还是狼人。Each night, checks if a player is Good or Evil.

女巫Witch＋

拥有一瓶解药和一瓶毒药。每晚至多使用一瓶，全程不可自救。One antidote and one poison. At most one per night. Cannot save herself.

剑客Swordsman＋

每晚可以选择一名玩家，若自己在当夜或次日死亡，次夜该名玩家出局。不能连续两晚选择同一玩家。Each night, designates a player: if the Swordsman dies that night or the next day, the designated player exits the following night. Cannot choose the same player on consecutive nights.

大天使Archangel＋

每晚可以选择一名玩家，该玩家免疫所有负面状态。不能连续两晚选择同一人。Each night, grants one player immunity to all negative effects. Cannot choose the same player on consecutive nights.

👤 村民
👤 Villager
平民 ×4Villager ×4＋

无特殊技能，凭直觉与逻辑找出狼人。No special ability. Use logic and instinct to find the wolves.

🐺 狼人
🐺 Werewolf
狼人 ×3Werewolf ×3＋

每晚共同击杀一名玩家。Each night, collectively eliminate one player.

梦魇Nightmare＋

每晚必须恐惧一名玩家，随后与狼人一同行动，不可自爆。被恐惧的玩家在当夜无法行动，不能连续两晚选择同一人。Each night, must terrorise one player (disabling their action) before joining the wolves. Cannot self-destruct. Cannot terrorise the same player on consecutive nights.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

天使梦魇为12人竞技版型，梦魇封锁神职行动，大天使护盾关键角色，剑客后手枪随时改变局面，以下为完整游戏流程。

A 12-player competitive template. Nightmare disables deity actions; Archangel shields key roles; the Swordsman's delayed shot can turn the tide. Below is the complete game flow.

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共12张：预言家、女巫、剑客、大天使各1张，梦魇1张，狼人3张，平民4张。

The host secretly deals 12 role cards: 1 Seer, 1 Witch, 1 Swordsman, 1 Archangel, 1 Nightmare, 3 Werewolves, and 4 Villagers.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

所有人闭眼。梦魇与狼人互相睁眼确认身份，梦魇选择恐惧一名玩家（该玩家当夜无法行动）。大天使选择一名玩家免疫所有负面状态。女巫查看刀口，预言家查验一名玩家，剑客选择指定目标。

All close eyes. Nightmare and wolves open to identify each other; Nightmare selects a player to terrorise (disabling their night action). Archangel grants one player immunity. Witch sees the kill; Seer checks one player; Swordsman designates a target.

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参选玩家投票选出警长。警长投票权重为1.5票，出局时可传递或销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff's vote counts as 1.5 and may pass or destroy the badge upon elimination.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。若剑客在当夜死亡，其指定目标将在下一夜出局。注意：被大天使保护的玩家免疫梦魇的恐惧效果及所有夜间负面技能。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement. If the Swordsman dies this night, their designated target will exit the following night. Note: Archangel's protected player is immune to Nightmare's terror and all other negative night effects.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

梦魇每晚封锁一名玩家的行动，可针对预言家或女巫造成致命干扰。剑客指定目标后，若剑客当夜或次日死亡，则该目标在次夜出局。大天使不可连续两晚选择同一人。梦魇不可自爆。

Nightmare disables one player per night — targeting the Seer or Witch can be devastating. If the Swordsman dies that night or the next day, their designated target exits the following night. Archangel cannot choose the same player on consecutive nights. Nightmare cannot self-destruct.

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
