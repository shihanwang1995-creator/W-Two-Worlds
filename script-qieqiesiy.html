☀️ 镇民☀️ Townsfolk
艺术家Artist＋

背景故事
「天啊！多么美妙的作品！我的作品……用你们的话怎么说来着……对，璀璨夺目！栩栩如生！没错！」

角色能力
每局游戏限一次，在白天时，你可以私下询问说书人一个是非问题，你会得知该问题的答案。

角色简介
艺术家可以向说书人提出任一是非问题。艺术家可以询问有关任何内容的问题，也可以随心所欲的遣词造句来提问。说书人需要诚实地回答「是」、「不是」，或「我不知道」。是否提问由艺术家决定，而非说书人。艺术家向说书人询问不是公开交流，因此其他玩家不能旁听。艺术家会秘密地询问并获取问题的答案。Information
"Mon Dieu! C'est lumineux! My work, she is... how you say... Magnifique! Dieu est révélé! Oui."

Summary
"Once per game, during the day, privately ask the Storyteller any yes/no question."
The Artist may ask any 1 question, and get an honest answer.
· The question may deal with anything at all, phrased in any way they want. The Storyteller honestly answers "yes," "no," or "I don't know."
· It is up to the Artist to talk with the Storyteller, not the other way around. This isn't a public conversation, and the group can't listen in. It's private.

气球驾驶员Balloonist＋

背景故事
「再来点热量！高点！高点！再高点！啊~从这上面往下看，风景真美，你说呢？你能看见孩子们在河边柳树下钓鱼吗？你能看到马戏团帐篷杆上反射的阳光吗？这是什么？一位老人孤身一人在葡萄园里昏倒了？少来点热量！低点！低点！再低点！」

角色能力
每个夜晚，你会得知一名与上个夜晚得知的玩家角色类型不同的玩家。[+0~1外来者]

角色简介
气球驾驶员能得知不同角色类型的玩家。气球驾驶员每次必须得知不同于上一次得知玩家的角色类型的玩家。气球驾驶员不会得知玩家的角色类型。向气球驾驶员展示的玩家可以存活或死亡，可以善良或邪恶。如果气球驾驶员中毒或醉酒，他可以得知与上一次得知玩家的角色类型相同的玩家。如果之后气球驾驶员恢复了健康清醒，他们必须得知与上一次得知玩家的角色类型不同的玩家。设置调整阶段，说书人可以决定是否让气球驾驶员添加一名额外的外来者。Information
"More heat! Higher! Higher! Più alto! Ahhh... it is so beautiful from up here, don't you agree? Can you see the children fishing by the river, under the willow? Can you see the glint of the sun on the circus tent-poles? What's this? An old man, alone, passed out in the vineyard? Less heat! Lower! Lower! Vai più in basso!"

Summary
"Each night, you learn a player of a different character type than last night. [+0 or +1 Outsider]"
The Balloonist learns players of different character types.
· Each time the Balloonist learns a player, the player must have a different character type to the previously shown player.
· The Balloonist does not learn the character type of the player they learn.
· The shown player can be alive or dead, and good or evil.
· If the Balloonist is drunk or poisoned, they may learn a character of the same type as the previously shown player. When sober and healthy, they must learn a player of a different character type.
· During setup, the Storyteller may choose to add an Outsider due to the Balloonist's ability.

渔夫Fisherman＋

背景故事
「这是这条河流里我最喜欢的一截儿……看看阳光是如何让一道彩虹横跨修道院和集市的？这是能让鱼儿长得肥美的最好的地方。我一天天变老，它们也一天天长大。」

角色能力
每局游戏限一次，在白天时，你可以让说书人给你一些能帮助你的阵营获胜的建议。

角色简介
渔夫知道其他人都无从得知的事情：该怎么做。渔夫玩家选择何时使用他的能力。当他拜访说书人时，说书人选择给渔夫什么样的建议。说书人给出的建议不一定是「事实」，而是说书人认为如果被采纳的话，能帮助渔夫获胜的战略性建议。如果渔夫醉酒或中毒，说书人可能会给渔夫不好的建议。Information
"This was my favourite part of the river... see how the sunlight makes a rainbow from the monastery to the market? This was the best place for big fish. And the older I get, the bigger they were."

Summary
"Once per game, during the day, visit the Storyteller for some advice to help your team win."
The Fisherman knows something that nobody else can know: what should be done.
· The Fisherman player chooses when to use their ability.
· When they visit the Storyteller, the Storyteller chooses what piece of advice to give the Fisherman.
· The Storyteller's pieces of advice are not necessarily "facts". They are strategy tips that the Storyteller believes will help the Fisherman win, if they are followed.
· If the Fisherman is drunk or poisoned, the Storyteller may give the Fisherman bad advice.

博学者Savant＋

背景故事
「地上有七十二根火柴……今天的太阳落得更早，月亮还是按时升起……一块破布……庄园里的邪恶之物……三个又三个……我们相信的事物和他真正看到的不一样……绿光代表镁……残留物，但是图案不太对……地上有七十二根火柴……」

角色能力
每个白天，你可以私下询问说书人以得知两条信息：一个是正确的，一个是错误的。

角色简介
博学者能得知惊人的信息，每一个白天，甚至每一局游戏里他能得到的信息都不会相同，但其中一半是错误的。每个白天，说书人选择两条信息提供给博学者——一条必须是正确的，一条必须是错误的，博学者不知道哪条对哪条错。是否与说书人交谈取决于博学者而非说书人。博学者从说书人处获取信息不是公开交流，博学者会秘密地获取信息。博学者可以选择不从说书人那里获取信息。醉酒或中毒的博学者可能会得到两条正确信息或两条错误信息。Information
"Seventy-two matchsticks on the floor... the sun sets early but the moon is unchanged... a torn piece of cloth... evil in the manor house... three by three... the one we trusted is not what he seems... green light means magnesium... residue, but the pattern is wrong... Seventy-two matchsticks on the floor..."

Summary
"Each day, you may visit the Storyteller to learn 2 things in private: 1 is true & 1 is false."
The Savant gets crazy, amazing information that is different every day and every game, but exactly half of it is completely false.
· Each day, the Storyteller chooses two pieces of information to give the Savant... so get creative! One must be true, and one must be false, and the Savant won't know which is which.
· It is up to the Savant to talk with the Storyteller, not the other way around. This isn't a public conversation, and the group can't listen in. It's private.
· The Savant can choose to not visit the Storyteller if they wish.
· A drunk or poisoned Savant might get two pieces of true information or two pieces of false information.

失忆者Amnesiac＋

背景故事
「等下，你说了啥？谁？哦，没事了。等下，你说了啥？」

角色能力
你不知道你的能力是什么。每个白天你可以找说书人猜测一次，你会得知你的猜测有多准确。

角色简介
失忆者不会知道他的能力是什么。由说书人来决定失忆者的具体能力。这可以是来自《血染钟楼》中现有角色的能力，也可以是与之相似的能力，或是完全原创的能力。失忆者可能会在夜晚的任意时刻被唤醒，以得知信息或者选择玩家。他的能力也可以是被动效果，无需失忆者玩家主动做出任何行动。每个白天，失忆者都可以私下拜访说书人，并猜测自己的能力是什么。说书人用以下方式回答：「无关」代表猜测与实际差距较大，「有关」代表猜测的方向正确，「接近」代表猜测的结果与实际能力较为接近，而「完美」代表猜测的结果与实际能力完全相符。Information
"Wait. What. Who? Oh, ok. Wait. What?"

Summary
"You do not know what your ability is. Each day, privately guess what it is: you learn how accurate you are."
The Amnesiac doesn't know their own ability.
· The Storyteller decides what the Amnesiac's ability is. It may be the same ability as another character in Blood On The Clocktower, something similar, or something original.
· The Amnesiac may wake at any time during the night to learn information or to choose a player, or their ability may be passive—not requiring action from the Amnesiac player.
· Each day, the Amnesiac talks to the Storyteller in private, and guesses what their ability is. The Storyteller answers "cold" if the guess is very wrong, "warm" if on the right track, "hot" if very close, and "bingo" if spot on.
· Their guess may be specific, such as "Am I learning two players each night that are the same alignment?", or vague, such as "Is my ability something to do with dead players?"

食人族Cannibal＋

背景故事
「我讨厌小丑的味道。吃起来会让我想笑。」

角色能力
你拥有上个死于处决的玩家的能力。如果该玩家属于邪恶阵营，你中毒直到下个善良玩家死于处决。

角色简介
食人族会吃下被处决的角色，并获得他们的能力。如果一名善良玩家死于处决，食人族能获得他的能力。如果一名邪恶玩家死于处决，食人族也只会认为自己获得了能力，因为他中毒了，说书人可能会对他撒谎。每当有一名玩家死于处决时，食人族会失去上一次获得的能力。处决一名已经死亡的玩家不会让食人族获得能力。处决一名存活但并未导致死亡的玩家也不会让食人族获得能力。玩家必须要被处决并因此死亡，才会让食人族获得能力。食人族不会被告知自己获得了什么能力。如果食人族获得了「即使你已死亡」的能力，例如陌客，或在死亡后才会生效的能力，例如守鸦人或心上人，食人族会在死亡时仍然保留这些能力，但失去食人族自己角色本身的能力。Information
"I don't like clowns. They taste funny."

Summary
"You have the ability of the recently killed executee. If they are evil, you are poisoned until a good player dies by execution."
The Cannibal eats executed characters, gaining their ability.
· If a good player dies by execution, the Cannibal gains that player's ability. If an evil player dies by execution, the Cannibal is poisoned and the Storyteller may be lying to them.
· Each time a player dies by execution, the Cannibal loses the ability of the previous player.
· Executing a dead player won't grant the Cannibal an ability. A player must be executed and die for the Cannibal to gain their ability.
· The Cannibal is not told which ability they have gained. They must figure that out for themselves.
· If the Cannibal has an "even if dead" ability, such as the Recluse, or an ability that implies it works while dead, such as the Ravenkeeper or Sweetheart, the Cannibal keeps that ability when they die, but loses their Cannibal ability.

🌫️ 外来者🌫️ Outsider
疯子Lunatic＋

背景故事
「吾即是暗夜……没错吧？」

角色能力
你以为你是一个恶魔，但其实你不是。恶魔知道你是疯子以及你在每个夜晚选择了哪些玩家。

角色简介
疯子以为自己是恶魔。和酒鬼很相似，疯子不知道自己的真实角色和真实阵营。疯子每个夜晚都会被唤醒来发动攻击，就如同他是场上真正的恶魔，但是疯子的选择没有效果，因为他没有恶魔的能力。疯子会在首个夜晚被唤醒来得知三个不在场的角色，以及与当前游戏数量符合的爪牙，但是这些信息可能是错误的。真正的恶魔会知道疯子每个夜晚攻击了哪些玩家。Information
"I am the night... I think."

Summary
"You think you are a Demon, but you are not. The Demon knows who you are & who you choose at night."
The Lunatic thinks that they are the Demon.
· Much like the Drunk, the Lunatic does not know their real character or real alignment. They are woken each night to attack as if they were the Demon that is in play, but their choices have no effect because they have no Demon ability.
· The Lunatic wakes during the first night to learn three bluffs and the appropriate number of Minions, but this information may be wrong.
· The real Demon knows which players the Lunatic chose to attack each night.

畸形秀演员Mutant＋

背景故事
「我不是怪物！我是人！求你了！」

角色能力
如果你「疯狂」地证明自己是外来者，你可能被处决。

角色简介
畸形秀演员如果试图揭露自己是谁，他就会被处决。「疯狂」是一个术语，意思是「你正在尝试说服他人相信某件事情」。说服的形式包含口头暗示自己的角色，也包含在自己的角色被质疑时的保持沉默。畸形秀演员是否「疯狂」由说书人来决定。如果你认为他正在以任何方式证明自己是一名外来者，你就可以处决他，即使在提名阶段之外，甚至是在晚上都可以处决他。如果你这样做，正常情况下这意味今天白天不会再有其他人被处决。如果畸形秀演员晚上暗示他是畸形秀演员，那么你在当晚可以处决他，即使今天白天已经有人被处决。宣布他的死亡，并正常进行夜晚。明天仍然可能发生一场处决。Information
"I am not a freak! I am a human being! Have mercy!"

Summary
"If you are 'mad' about being an Outsider, you might be executed."
The Mutant is killed if they try to reveal who they are.
· "Madness" is a term that means "you are trying to convince the group of something." If the Mutant player is mad about being an Outsider, this means they are trying to convince people that they are an Outsider.
· This can be by verbally hinting who they are, or by their silence when questioned. It is always up to the Storyteller to decide what the Mutant is doing. If you think they are trying to convince the group they are an Outsider in any way, you can execute them—even outside the nomination phase, or at night.
· If the Mutant hints that they are the Mutant at night, you may execute them that night, even if an execution happened today. Declare they have died, and continue the night phase as normal. An execution may still happen the next day.

🩸 爪牙🩸 Minion
寡妇Widow＋

背景故事
「还要再来点酒吗？麦角酒庄的这款07年的窖藏非常特别。是的，的的确确很特别。」

角色能力
在你的首个夜晚，你能查看魔典并选择一名玩家：他中毒。随后，始终会有一名善良玩家知道寡妇在场。

角色简介
寡妇查看魔典并毒害一名她选择的玩家。寡妇仅在她的首个夜晚行动并使一名玩家中毒。因寡妇中毒的玩家会一直保持中毒直到寡妇死亡。在寡妇行动的那个夜晚，会有一名善良玩家知道寡妇在场，他不会得知谁是寡妇，也不会得知谁中毒了。Information
"More wine? Château d'Ergot '07 is a very special vintage. My yes, very special indeed."

Summary
"On your 1st night, look at the Grimoire & choose a player: they are poisoned. 1 good player knows a Widow is in play."
The Widow sees the Grimoire and poisons a character of their choice.
· The Widow acts on their first night only, poisoning one player.
· The player that the Widow poisons is poisoned until the Widow dies.
· On the same night that the Widow acts, one good player learns that the Widow is in play, but not which player is the Widow, and not which player is poisoned.

哥布林Goblin＋

背景故事
「你不会想要侮辱哥布林的。你真的，真的不会。让我们换个话题……我能再吃一块蛋糕吗？」

角色能力
如果你在被提名后公开声明自己是哥布林且在那个白天被处决，你的阵营获胜。

角色简介
哥布林会在小镇明知他是哥布林却要处决他时采取报复。如果哥布林被处决，邪恶阵营获胜。为了让这种情况发生，哥布林需要在被提名后、开始投票前告知其他玩家他是哥布林，且需要让所有人都听见。如果哥布林没有公布自己的角色就被处决了，那么哥布林死亡，游戏如常进行。哥布林必须在被处决的当天宣布自己的角色，只有这样他的能力才会生效。在前一天或更早的时间里宣布不会产生效果。任何玩家都可以在被提名时声称自己是哥布林。Information
"You don't want to insult the goblins. You really, really don't. On a completely different note… can I have another piece of cake?"

Summary
"If you publicly claim to be the Goblin when nominated & are executed that day, your team wins."
The Goblin takes revenge if the town knowingly executes them.
· If the Goblin is executed, evil wins.
· For this to happen the Goblin needs to tell the group that they are the Goblin when nominated, before votes happen, and in a way that everyone hears. The good players need to know the risk.
· If the Goblin is executed without telling the group when nominated, the Goblin dies and the game continues as normal.
· The Goblin must have claimed to be the Goblin today for their ability to work. Telling the group yesterday doesn't count.
· Any player may claim to be the Goblin when nominated.

😈 恶魔😈 Demon
利维坦Leviathan＋

背景故事
「末日将临，斗争永无止境。地狱之心，吾以利剑相迎。仇恨燃尽，然而愤懑难平。」

角色能力
如果多于一名善良玩家被处决，邪恶阵营获胜。所有玩家都知道利维坦在场。在第五个白天结束时，邪恶阵营获胜。

角色简介
利维坦不进行任何杀戮。所有玩家都会知道利维坦在场，即使利维坦在游戏过程中被创造也是如此。可以有任意数量的邪恶玩家被处决，然而如果有多于一名善良玩家被处决，邪恶阵营获胜。被处决的角色是什么无关紧要，只看那名玩家在被处决的时候的阵营是什么。当第五个白天结束时，如果利维坦仍然存活，邪恶阵营获胜。不论何种类型的处决均会计算在内，即使处决没有让玩家死亡也会正常计算。善良玩家被处决但因为和平主义者的能力仍然存活也会计算在内。Information
"To the last, I grapple with thee. From Hell's heart, I stab at thee. For hate's sake, I spit my last breath at thee."

Summary
"If more than 1 good player is executed, evil wins. All players know you are in play. After day 5, evil wins."
The Leviathan doesn't kill.
· All players know the Leviathan is in play, even if the Leviathan is created mid-game.
· Any number of evil players may be executed, but if more than one good player is executed, evil wins. It doesn't matter which characters were executed, only the alignment of the player at the time they were executed.
· When the fifth day ends and night begins, if the Leviathan is still alive, evil wins.
· All types of execution count, even if the player doesn't die. A player executed due to the Virgin, or due to revealing that they are the Mutant, is still executed. An executed player who lives due to the Pacifist is still executed.
