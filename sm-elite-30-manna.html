Synopsis

1914年（民国三年）9月28日，上海郊外。

在上海郊外，有一座建于峭壁之上的"谭公馆"，背靠大海，西侧有通往海滩的小路，南面是大路。"谭公馆"共有三层，每层室内高两米有余，通电后悬挂起西洋吊灯，二层与三层都有向西与临海的内嵌阳台（有一米多高的围栏），共有五间装有浴室的房间，一层的大厅联通二层（二层中间也有一米多高的围栏），可以从二层走廊上俯视大厅。

September 28th, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). Outskirts of Shanghai.

On the outskirts of Shanghai, perched on a clifftop overlooking the sea, stands "Tan Manor." Its western side has a path leading down to the beach; to the south lies the main road. The manor has three floors, each with ceilings over two metres high, fitted with electric Western-style chandeliers. The second and third floors each have recessed sea-view balconies with railings over a metre high, and five rooms with private bathrooms. The ground-floor hall connects to the second floor via an open landing from which the hall below can be overlooked.
