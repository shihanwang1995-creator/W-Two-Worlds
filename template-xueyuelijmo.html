Blood Moon · Demon Hunter

OVERVIEW · 简介
OVERVIEW

模板简介

Overview

血月使徒自爆可触发血月封禁所有神职；猎魔人每晚狩猎，猜错则自身出局，高风险高回报。

Blood Moon Apostle's self-destruct triggers a Blood Moon disabling all deities; Demon Hunter hunts nightly — guess wrong and they exit. High risk, high reward.

✨ 神职✨ Deity
预言家Seer＋

每晚可以查验一名玩家的身份是好人还是狼人。Each night, check whether a player is Good or Evil.

女巫Witch＋

拥有一瓶解药和一瓶毒药，持有解药时可看到狼人当夜刀口。每晚至多使用一瓶药，全程不可自救。Holds one antidote and one poison. When holding the antidote, sees the wolf kill target. At most one potion per night. Cannot save herself.

白痴Fool＋

被放逐出局后可以翻牌，失去警徽拥有权。翻牌后仍被视为存活神职，投票时记为0.5票。After being exiled, may flip their card and lose badge rights. Still counts as a living deity worth 0.5 votes.

猎魔人Demon Hunter＋

从第二夜起，每晚可以选择一名玩家狩猎。若该玩家为狼人则其出局；若该玩家为好人则猎魔人出局。From night two, each night may hunt one player. If Evil, they exit; if Good, the Demon Hunter exits.

👥 平民👥 Villager
平民Villager＋

无特殊技能，凭直觉与逻辑找出狼人。No special ability. Use logic and instinct to identify the wolves.

🐺 狼人阵营🐺 Wolf Team
狼人Werewolf＋

每晚可以共同击杀一名玩家。白天可以自爆，pk发言除外。Each night, collectively eliminate one player. May self-destruct during the day (not during duel/last words).

血月使徒Blood Moon Apostle＋

与狼队一同行动，可以自爆。如果血月使徒在白天自爆，则当夜为血月，血月中所有神职技能被封禁。当狼队仅剩血月使徒存活时，每晚可以击杀两名玩家。Acts with wolves, may self-destruct. If self-destructs during the day, that night is a Blood Moon — all deity skills disabled. When last wolf standing, may kill two players per night.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共12张：预言家 · 女巫 · 白痴 · 猎魔人 · 血月使徒 · 狼人×3 · 平民×4。

The host secretly deals 12 role cards: Seer · Witch · Fool · Demon Hunter · Blood Moon Apostle · Werewolf×3 · Villager×4.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

所有人闭眼。血月使徒与狼人睁眼互认，商定首刀目标。女巫查看刀口。预言家查验一名玩家。从第二夜起，猎魔人选择一名玩家狩猎：若该玩家为狼人则其出局；若为好人则猎魔人出局。

All close eyes. Blood Moon Apostle and wolves open and select a kill. Witch sees the kill. Seer checks one player. From night two, Demon Hunter selects a player to hunt — if Evil, they exit; if Good, the Demon Hunter exits.

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参与竞选的玩家投票选出警长。警长发言顺序享有优先权，投票时拥有1.5票权重。警长出局时可将警徽传递给任意存活玩家，或选择销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff gains priority in speaking order and their vote counts as 1.5. Upon elimination, the Sheriff may pass the badge to any surviving player — or destroy it.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

若血月使徒在白天自爆，当夜为血月，所有神职技能被封禁。白痴若被放逐可翻牌保留0.5票权，仍视为存活神职。猎魔人仅限第二夜起行动。

If Blood Moon Apostle self-destructs during the day, that night is a Blood Moon — all deity skills disabled. Fool may flip on exile, retaining 0.5 votes and counted as a living deity. Demon Hunter acts from night two onward.

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
