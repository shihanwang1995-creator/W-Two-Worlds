剧情简介

Synopsis

民国三年，天津。一位名为耿通贤的天津商贾，凭借时代动乱让商铺起死回生，近几年突然暴富，接连盘下数家商铺，更买下前清贵族"马佳氏"的旧邸"槿园"。

最让人津津乐道的，是槿园内原有一位格格，原本许配给耿通贤之子耿浩贞，最后却成了耿通贤的妻子，并继续居于槿园。

不过任谁都没想到，就在这贵族院内一条人命悄然之间香消玉损，一个故事也因此展开……

阎王请也，从前罪业，待交谁替？

Year 3 of the Republic of China. Tianjin. A merchant named 耿通贤 turned the chaos of a changing era into sudden fortune, acquiring several businesses and purchasing "Jǐn Garden" — the former residence of the Qing noble clan "Majia."

What tongues wagged about most, however, was the young princess who had lived in the garden — originally betrothed to his son 耿浩贞, she ended up becoming 耿通贤's own wife, and continued to reside within its walls.

Then, without warning, a life was quietly extinguished within these noble halls — and a story began to unfold…
