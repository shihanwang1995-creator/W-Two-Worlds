Synopsis

戏剧，是灵魂的交融。而语言，是恶魔的低吟。它欺骗你，腐蚀你，直至你体无完肤。伟大的表演者，注定是沉默的精灵。来到我的国度，许你悲喜与痴醉、癫狂与不羁。但唯独，不许说话…

Theatre is the fusion of souls. And language — language is the devil's murmur. It deceives you, corrodes you, until there is nothing left of you. A great performer is destined to be a spirit of silence. Come to my kingdom, and I will grant you sorrow and joy, obsession and madness, wildness and freedom. But one thing alone is forbidden — you must not speak…
