OVERVIEW · 简介
OVERVIEW

模板简介

Overview

觉醒丘比特首夜通过司仪选定两名情侣，情侣阵营强制第三方。情侣中一人出局，另一人立即跟随，局势瞬间改变。

Cupid designates two lovers via a Officiant on night one — lovers form a forced third-party faction. One lover exits, the other follows immediately.

✨ 神职✨ Deity
预言家Seer＋

每晚可以查验一名玩家的身份是好人还是狼人。Each night, check whether a player is Good or Evil.

女巫Witch＋

拥有一瓶解药和一瓶毒药，持有解药时可看到狼人当夜刀口。每晚至多使用一瓶药，全程不可自救。Holds one antidote and one poison. When holding the antidote, sees the wolf kill target. At most one potion per night. Cannot save herself.

猎人Hunter＋

出局时可带走一名存活玩家。被毒不可开枪。When eliminated, may take one surviving player. Cannot fire if poisoned.

守卫Guard＋

每晚可以守护一名玩家，该玩家免疫狼刀。不能连续两晚守护同一名玩家。Each night, protect one player from the wolf kill. Cannot protect the same player two nights in a row.

炸弹人Bomber＋

被放逐出局时，可以选择翻牌并带走所有本轮投票给自己的人。When exiled, may flip their card and take all players who voted for them.

👥 平民👥 Villager
平民Villager＋

无特殊技能，凭直觉与逻辑找出狼人。No special ability. Use logic and instinct to identify the wolves.

🎭 第三方🎭 Third Party
觉醒丘比特Cupid＋

首夜选择任意一名玩家作为司仪，之后司仪单独睁眼选择两名玩家成为情侣。无论司仪与情侣的原本身份是什么，丘比特、司仪、情侣强制组成第三方阵营。当情侣中的任意一方因任何方式出局时，另一方立即跟随出局。On night one, selects an Officiant who then chooses two players to become lovers. Cupid, Officiant, and lovers form a mandatory third-party faction. When either lover exits, the other immediately follows.

🐺 狼人阵营🐺 Wolf Team
狼人Werewolf＋

每晚可以共同击杀一名玩家。白天可以自爆，pk发言除外。Each night, collectively eliminate one player. May self-destruct during the day (not during duel/last words).

狼王Wolf King＋

出局时可带走一名存活玩家。被毒或梦游不可开枪。When eliminated, may take one surviving player. Cannot fire if poisoned or sleepwalking.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共16张：预言家 · 女巫 · 猎人 · 守卫 · 炸弹人 · 平民×5 · 狼王 · 狼人×4 · 觉醒丘比特。

The host secretly deals 16 role cards: Seer · Witch · Hunter · Guard · Bomber · Villager×5 · Wolf King · Werewolf×4 · Cupid.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

首夜：觉醒丘比特选择一名玩家作为司仪，随后司仪单独睁眼选择两名玩家成为情侣。丘比特、司仪、情侣强制组成第三方阵营，无论原本身份是什么。情侣中任意一方出局时，另一方立即跟随出局。

Night one: Cupid selects an Officiant; the Officiant then opens alone and selects two players to become lovers. Cupid, Officiant, and lovers form a mandatory third-party faction regardless of their original roles. When either lover exits, the other immediately follows.

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参与竞选的玩家投票选出警长。警长发言顺序享有优先权，投票时拥有1.5票权重。警长出局时可将警徽传递给任意存活玩家，或选择销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff gains priority in speaking order and their vote counts as 1.5. Upon elimination, the Sheriff may pass the badge to any surviving player — or destroy it.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

本局为屠城规则。炸弹人被放逐出局时，可翻牌带走所有本轮投票给自己的人。狼王出局时可带走一名存活玩家（被毒不可开枪）。

This template uses massacre rules. Bomber, if exiled, may flip to take all players who voted for them. Wolf King drags a player on exit (cannot fire if poisoned).

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
