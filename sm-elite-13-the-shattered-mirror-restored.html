Synopsis

1914年（民国三年）9月12日，天津。

"讨袁之役"一败涂地后，北洋政府分赴各省搜捕"反袁"成员，与此同时，海外"讨袁会"不断策划暗杀"保袁"势力。天津城外西郊，有一户"林家"，大院重楼，远离人烟。家主林居尚曾南下闯荡，后来衣锦还乡，如今即将年满五十，生辰在即。

这本是大喜之事，林居尚却在今天一早就坐车前往城里的"娘娘宫"要寻一位"算命先生"指点迷津……

September 12th, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). Tianjin.

In the aftermath of the failed "Campaign Against Yuan," the Beiyang government has been dispatching agents across the provinces to hunt down anti-Yuan members, while overseas dissidents have been plotting the assassination of pro-Yuan figures. On the western outskirts of Tianjin lies the Lin family estate — a grand compound of high buildings, set apart from the world. Its patriarch, 林居尚, once went south to make his fortune, and returned home a wealthy man. He is about to turn fifty, and his birthday celebrations are imminent.

Yet on this auspicious morning, 林居尚 has set off by car to visit a fortune teller at the Niangniangmiao temple in the city…
