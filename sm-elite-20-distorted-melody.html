Synopsis

1914年（民国三年）9月21日，镇江，白鸟河畔。

沪宁铁路沿途经过的镇江，早在1861年就被英国人开辟租界，1911年又被英租界工部局占据了江边马路、设置栅栏。镇江附近地势西高东低，河流众多，其中一条因每年有白色候鸟驻留，被称作"白鸟河"。

在"白鸟河"南有一座西式小楼，里面住着一位寡居的阮太太，常年出租"阮宅"空屋……

September 21st, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). Zhenjiang — beside the White Bird River.

Zhenjiang was opened as a British concession as early as 1861, with the concession's Municipal Council later seizing the riverside road. One of its many rivers, frequented each year by white migratory birds, is known as the White Bird River.

On the southern bank stands a Western-style villa. Its widowed owner, Madam Ruan, has long rented out the spare rooms of "Ruan House" to lodgers…
