Templates

多种模板均经过精心设计，各具独特玩法与策略深度，由专业主持人全程带场。

Multiple templates, each carefully crafted with its own strategic depth and unique mechanics — guided by a professional host throughout.

⚔️

预女猎白

Seer · Witch · Hunter · Fool

经典四神配置，神民平衡稳健。愚者翻牌机制带来额外变数，适合追求纯粹推理博弈的玩家。

Classic four-deity balance. The Fool's flip mechanic adds a crucial twist — ideal for pure deduction seekers.

🪦

石像守墓

Gargoyle · Gravedigger

守墓人从放逐信息中获取情报，石像鬼带来独特的第三方变数，信息链错综复杂，步步惊心。

The Gravedigger reads exile results for clues; the Gargoyle adds a wild third-party twist. Every step is treacherous.

🤖

机械通灵

Mechano · Medium

通灵师精确查验身份，机械狼伪装复制技能混入神职。好人需在海量信息中甄别真假，步步为营。

The Medium verifies precisely; the Mechano Wolf copies roles to blend in. Sift through noise to expose the impostor.

🛡️

机械装甲

Mechano · Armour

双重伪装狼同时潜伏，通灵师的查验结果真假难辨。狼队拥有强大的容错与反制能力，极具挑战。

Two disguised wolves lurk at once, making every Medium reading suspect. The wolf team has extraordinary resilience.

🧵

唯邻是从

Neighbour's Puppet

首夜狼队将邻座好人转化为傀儡，傀儡本人毫不知情。好人阵营须在不知道谁是傀儡的情况下找出真正的威胁。

Night one, wolves convert an adjacent player into a puppet — unknown to the puppet. Good must find the threat without knowing who's been turned.

🎰

孤注一掷

All or Nothing

典狱长每夜设下囚笼，迫使两名玩家在交易与背叛之间抉择。每一次囚徒困境都可能改变战局走向。

The Warden sets a nightly cage — cooperate or betray. Every prisoner's dilemma can flip the game entirely.

🌙

骑士之夜

Knight's Vigil

骑士白天决斗揪狼，大天使护佑关键角色，黑夜使者庇护狼队反制神职。攻守博弈激烈，刀刀致命。

Knight duels by day; Archangel shields the vital; Night Messenger counters divine skills. An intense clash of attack and defence.

🏹

愚言围猎

Fool's Hunt

双猎人枪火交织，狼王出局必带人，愚者翻牌扰乱视线。好人攻势凌厉，狼队压力极大。

Crossed fire from two Hunters, a Wolf King who drags someone on exit, and the Fool muddying the waters. Wolves are under relentless pressure.

💤

黄粱美梦

A Dreamer's Gambit

催眠师每夜干扰狼队行动，瞌睡鬼以猜测代替猎杀，梦与现实的边界在此模糊，信息真假难辨。

The Hypnotist disrupts wolf actions nightly; the Sleepwalker kills through guesswork. Dream and reality blur — nothing is reliable.

😇

天使梦魇

Angel's Nightmare

梦魇每夜强制梦游一名玩家令其免疫狼刀，大天使保护关键神职，剑客的后手枪随时改变局面。天使与恶魔的终极对决。

Nightmare forces sleepwalking immunity nightly; the Archangel shields vital roles; the Swordsman's delayed shot can change everything.

典狱长Warden＋

首夜与狼人一同行动，全程不可自爆。从第二夜起率先行动，选择两名玩家进入囚笼，之后与狼人一同行动。典狱内的两人互相确认号码后决定交易或背叛：双方交易则均免于夜间伤害；双方背叛则技能目标互相转移；手势不同则交易者出局。典狱长在囚笼内时，两名囚犯均免疫狼刀和毒药，手势相同则典狱长出局，不同则另一方出局。

Acts with wolves on night one; cannot self-destruct. From night two, acts first — chooses two players for the cage. Caged players reveal numbers and choose to cooperate or betray: both cooperate = both immune to night kills; both betray = skill targets swap; different choices = the cooperating player exits. When the Warden is in the cage, both prisoners are immune to kills and poison.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏基本规则

Game Rules Overview

狼人隐藏在村民之中，善恶双方斗智斗勇，直到分出胜负。

Werewolves hide among the villagers — Good and Evil battle wits until one side prevails.

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密为每位玩家发放身份牌，身份分为三类：狼人、村民与神职。狼人夜间猎杀、白天伪装；村民凭直觉推理；神职拥有特殊能力，是好人阵营的核心。

The host secretly deals role cards. Roles fall into three types: Werewolf, Villager, and Deity. Wolves hunt by night and deceive by day; Villagers rely on instinct and logic; Deities wield special powers as the backbone of the Good team.

02
黑夜行动
Night Phase

所有玩家闭眼，狼人睁眼商定猎杀目标，特殊职业依次行动，各自秘密完成夜间技能。

All players close their eyes. Werewolves open theirs to select a kill target; special roles then act in sequence, each completing their night ability in secret.

03
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言，信息与误导从这一刻开始在场上蔓延。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased players may leave a final statement — information and misdirection begin spreading from this moment.

04
白天讨论
Day Discussion

所有存活玩家发言讨论，分享情报或制造谎言。每一句话都可能是线索，也可能是陷阱。

All surviving players speak and debate — sharing clues or spinning lies. Every word could be evidence or a trap.

05
投票淘汰
Vote & Exile

讨论结束后全体投票，得票最多的玩家被淘汰出局。

After discussion, players vote. The most-voted player is eliminated.

06
胜利条件
Victory Conditions

村民阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营需淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

Villagers win by eliminating all werewolves. Werewolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles. Every vote is decisive.
