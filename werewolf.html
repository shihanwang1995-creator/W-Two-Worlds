HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏基本规则

Game Rules Overview

狼人隐藏在村民之中，善恶双方斗智斗勇，直到分出胜负。

Werewolves hide among the villagers — Good and Evil battle wits until one side prevails.

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密为每位玩家发放身份牌，身份分为三类：狼人、村民与神职。狼人夜间猎杀、白天伪装；村民凭直觉推理；神职拥有特殊能力，是好人阵营的核心。

The host secretly deals role cards. Roles fall into three types: Werewolf, Villager, and Deity. Wolves hunt by night and deceive by day; Villagers rely on instinct and logic; Deities wield special powers as the backbone of the Good team.

02
黑夜行动
Night Phase

所有玩家闭眼，狼人睁眼商定猎杀目标，特殊职业依次行动，各自秘密完成夜间技能。

All players close their eyes. Werewolves open theirs to select a kill target; special roles then act in sequence, each completing their night ability in secret.

03
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言，信息与误导从这一刻开始在场上蔓延。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased players may leave a final statement — information and misdirection begin spreading from this moment.

04
白天讨论
Day Discussion

所有存活玩家发言讨论，分享情报或制造谎言。每一句话都可能是线索，也可能是陷阱。

All surviving players speak and debate — sharing clues or spinning lies. Every word could be evidence or a trap.

05
投票淘汰
Vote & Exile

讨论结束后全体投票，得票最多的玩家被淘汰出局。

After discussion, players vote. The most-voted player is eliminated.

06
胜利条件
Victory Conditions

村民阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营需淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

Villagers win by eliminating all werewolves. Werewolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles. Every vote is decisive.
