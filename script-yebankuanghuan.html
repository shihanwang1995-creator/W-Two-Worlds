夜半狂欢

The Midnight Oasis

👥 7-15人 👥 7-15 Players ⭐⭐ 进阶

在月色朦胧的深夜，古老的钟楼仿佛被一层神秘的面纱所笼罩，阴森而幽邃。此时，钟楼的大门悄然洞开，一群各怀鬼胎的宾客悄然涌入，他们身负着不为人知的秘密，踏入了这场注定不平凡的夜半狂欢。究竟是哈迪寂亚、亡骨魔还是可恶的说书人在作祟？《夜半狂欢》充满了可以干扰镇民的角色，但是真正制造混乱的到底是谁？ An Al-Hadikhia, a Vigormortis, or a dastardly Storyteller is at bay, and it's the town's job to figure out which. The Midnight Oasis is chock full of characters that can mess with the townsfolk, but who's doing the messing?
☀️ 镇民
☀️ Townsfolk
贵族Noble＋

待补充You start knowing 3 players, 1 and only 1 of which is evil.

气球师Balloonist＋

待补充Each night, you learn a player of a different character type than last night. [+0 or +1 Outsider]

舞蛇人Snake Charmer＋

待补充Each night, choose an alive player: a chosen Demon swaps characters & alignments with you & is then poisoned.

博学者Savant＋

待补充Each day, you may visit the Storyteller to learn 2 things in private: 1 is true & 1 is false.

失忆者Amnesiac＋

待补充You do not know what your ability is. Each day, privately guess what it is: you learn how accurate you are.

工程师Engineer＋

待补充Once per game, at night, choose which Minions or which Demon is in play.

猎人Huntsman＋

待补充Once per game, at night, choose a living player: the Damsel, if chosen, becomes a not-in-play Townsfolk. [+the Damsel]

教授Professor＋

待补充Once per game, at night*, choose a dead player: if they are a Townsfolk, they are resurrected.

渔夫Fisherman＋

待补充Once per game, during the day, visit the Storyteller for some advice to help your team win.

农夫Farmer＋

待补充When you die at night, an alive good player becomes a Farmer.

罂粟种植者Poppy Grower＋

待补充Minions & Demons do not know each other. If you die, they learn who each other are that night.

食人族Cannibal＋

待补充You have the ability of the recently killed executee. If they are evil, you are poisoned until a good player dies by execution.

无神论者Atheist＋

待补充The Storyteller can break the game rules, and if executed, good wins, even if you are dead. [No evil characters]

🌫️ 外来者
🌫️ Outsider
少女Damsel＋

待补充All Minions know a Damsel is in play. If a Minion publicly guesses you (once), your team loses.

酒鬼Drunk＋

待补充You do not know you are the Drunk. You think you are a Townsfolk character, but you are not.

石像Golem＋

待补充You may only nominate once per game. When you do, if the nominee is not the Demon, they die.

理发师Barber＋

待补充If you died today or tonight, the Demon may choose 2 players (not another Demon) to swap characters.

🩸 爪牙
🩸 Minion
梅泽费勒斯Mezepheles＋

待补充You start knowing a secret word. The 1st good player to say this word becomes evil that night.

投毒者Poisoner＋

待补充Each night, choose a player: they are poisoned tonight and tomorrow day.

麻脸巫婆Pit-Hag＋

待补充Each night*, choose a player & a character they become (if not in play). If a Demon is made, deaths tonight are arbitrary.

心理变态者Psychopath＋

待补充Each day, before nominations, you may publicly choose a player: they die. If executed, you only die if you lose roshambo.

😈 恶魔
😈 Demon
哈迪寂亚Al-Hadikhia＋

待补充Each night*, you may choose 3 players (all players learn who): each silently chooses to live or die, but if all live, all die.

亡骨魔Vigormortis＋

待补充Each night*, choose a player: they die. Minions you kill keep their ability & poison 1 Townsfolk neighbor. [-1 Outsider]
