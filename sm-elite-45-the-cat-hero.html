Synopsis

1914年（民国三年）10月10日，山西汾河西岸，襄陵，高家酿酒坊。

自从宋代发明了蒸馏设备，就有人尝试制作蒸馏酒，此后历经千余年，由黄酒演变、发展出白酒……明清以后，北方的白酒发展迅速，逐步代替了黄酒生产，早在唐代就已著名的汾州美酒更是成为蒸馏白酒的代表，许多酿酒世家不断改良工艺，留下许多不外传之秘密。

在汾河西岸的襄陵，有一户以酿酒闻名的"高家"，其宅高墙大院，东院内有专门的酿酒坊和两间酒库，晚上会在每间房门上挂一盏灯笼，里面的烛光只能照到房门附近……第五代继承人高守信除了祖传秘方，还不断尝试新技术，与妻子育有一双儿女，最上心的莫过于子女成家——儿子已有心仪对象，却在去年（1913年）出了一场意外……

October 10th, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). Xiangling, on the western bank of the Fen River, Shanxi — the Gao family distillery.

Since the Song dynasty's invention of distillation equipment, craftsmen have been refining the art of spirits. Over more than a thousand years, rice wine evolved into baijiu… After the Ming and Qing dynasties, northern baijiu flourished, gradually superseding the older brewing traditions. The famous Fenzhou liquor, celebrated since the Tang dynasty, became the defining example of distilled spirits — and many family distilleries jealously guarded their secret techniques across generations.

In Xiangling on the western bank of the Fen River stands the Gao family, renowned for their spirits. Their estate features a dedicated distillery and two storehouses in the eastern courtyard, with a lantern hung at each door each night — its candlelight barely reaching beyond the doorway… The fifth-generation heir 高守信 has built upon the ancestral recipe while embracing new techniques. He and his wife have a son and a daughter. His greatest wish is to see them settled — his son had already found someone he loved, but last year (1913), something went terribly wrong…
