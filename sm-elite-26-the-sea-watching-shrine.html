Synopsis

1914年（民国三年）9月7日，广东韶关西南，望海村。

在广东韶关西南，有一个"望海村"，村南的"望海祠"中，供奉着一尊"望海神"——"望海神"的来历早已无人知晓，几十年来，人们已经习惯用村名来称呼。

中元节后的第二天，"望海祠"的主人王濡寿被人发现死在祠中内院——当时院门从里镇上，等到破门之后，众人除了看到院内尚有余温的死者尸体，还看到地上有四个未干的血字……而这仅仅是随后一连串"自杀诅咒"的序幕！

September 7th, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). Wanghai Village, southwest of Shaoguan, Guangdong.

In the southwest outskirts of Shaoguan lies Wanghai Village. Within its southern shrine — the "Sea-Watching Shrine" — stands a deity whose origins have long been forgotten. For decades, the villagers have simply used the village name to refer to it.

On the day after the Ghost Festival, the shrine's master 王濡寿 was found dead inside the inner courtyard — the gate bolted from within. When the door was finally broken down, those who entered found not only his still-warm body, but four characters written in blood on the ground, not yet dry… This was only the prelude to a series of cursed "suicides" that would follow.
