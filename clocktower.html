HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏基本规则

Game Rules Overview

血染钟楼轮流经历黑夜与白天两个阶段，善恶双方各展所能，直到分出胜负。

Blood on the Clocktower alternates between Night and Day phases as Good and Evil battle for supremacy — until one side claims victory.

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

说书人秘密为每位玩家分配身份牌。玩家分为善良阵营（镇民与外来者）和邪恶阵营（爪牙与恶魔），各自拥有独特能力。

The Storyteller secretly assigns each player a role. Players are divided into Good (Townsfolk & Outsiders) and Evil (Minions & Demon) — each with unique abilities.

02
黑夜行动
Night Phase

夜晚所有玩家闭眼，各职业按顺序秘密行动——恶魔猎杀目标，特殊职业从说书人处获取情报。

All players close their eyes. Roles act in sequence — the Demon hunts while special roles receive information from the Storyteller.

03
白天讨论
Day Phase

玩家公开讨论，分享或伪造情报，通过投票处决最可疑的玩家。信任与欺骗在此刻正面交锋。

Players discuss openly, sharing or fabricating information. A vote decides who to execute — trust and deception collide head-on.

04
死亡与幽灵
Death & Ghosts

死亡玩家成为幽灵，仍可发言参与讨论，但每局仅剩一票。死亡不代表出局——你的信息依然关键。

Dead players become Ghosts — still able to speak, but with only one vote remaining. Death is not the end; your information still matters.

05
胜利条件
Victory Conditions

善良阵营成功处决恶魔即获胜；恶魔阵营则需将存活玩家减至仅剩2人时获胜。每一票都可能是终局。

Good wins by executing the Demon. Evil wins when only 2 players remain alive. Every vote could be the decisive one.

06
说书人裁决
The Storyteller

说书人全程掌控游戏节奏，为特定角色提供真实或虚假信息，确保每局公平、戏剧化且独一无二。

The Storyteller controls the flow of the game — feeding true or false information to certain roles, ensuring every session is fair, dramatic, and unique.
