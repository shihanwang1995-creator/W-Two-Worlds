Synopsis

你是徘徊于世间，最后的死亡回响。

大山的深处，有一座名为怨鬼村的隐世山村。

传说村子里有着被神明封印的妖邪——怨鬼。怨鬼的存在，会让亡者的灵魂无法安息，成为徘徊在世间的怨灵，破坏生与死的平衡。在神明的庇佑下，人们将怨鬼封印，获得了安宁的生活。

然而，村中唯一的阴阳师山背家族的灭亡，让封印出现了问题，导致原本用于看管封印的神社，成为了怨灵徘徊的鬼蜮。

为了安抚神社中的怨灵，村民们开始了每年一次的赎罪仪式。在赎罪的仪式上，此前死亡的某位亡者之魂将被唤回。在死者那残存于世间的回响中，真相将被揭开，凶手将在死亡符咒所引导的神罚中被处决。

You are the last death resonance, still wandering this world.

Deep within the mountains lies a hidden village known as Vengeful Spirit Village.

Legend holds that the village harbours a sealed evil — a Vengeful Demon — imprisoned by the gods. The Vengeful Demon's existence prevents the souls of the dead from finding rest, turning them into lingering wraiths that disrupt the balance between life and death. Under divine protection, the villagers sealed the demon away and lived in peace.

But when the only onmyoji family in the village — the Yamashiro clan — met their end, cracks appeared in the seal. The shrine once tasked with guarding it became a haunt for restless spirits.

To appease the wraiths in the shrine, the villagers began holding an annual atonement ritual. During the ritual, the soul of someone who recently died is summoned back. Through the echoes that the dead leave behind in the living world, the truth is revealed — and the killer is executed under divine punishment guided by the death curse.
