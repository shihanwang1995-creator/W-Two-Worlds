Synopsis

公元1914年（民国三年）8月18日。在上海之东的大洋上，有一座名为"孽岛"的小岛，岛南的居民大多聚住村落里，生活贫苦。岛北海边有一座二层建筑——原本是西洋人的疗养院，后被改建为"海砂馆"。

今天，岛南的人很少接近这里，却有许多戴着"面具"的客人乘船至此，应邀前往一向少有访客的"海砂馆"……

August 18th, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). In the ocean east of Shanghai lies a small island known as "Nie Island." Most of its southern inhabitants live in a poor fishing village, dependent on ferries to the mainland. On the island's northern shore stands a two-storey building — once a Western sanatorium, later converted into the "Haisha Pavilion."

Today, the islanders keep their distance — yet a group of guests in masks have arrived by boat, invited to the rarely-visited Haisha Pavilion…
