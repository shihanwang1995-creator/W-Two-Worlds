模板简介

Overview

典狱长每夜设下囚笼，迫使两名玩家在交易与背叛之间抉择。每一次囚徒困境都可能改变战局走向。

The Warden sets a nightly cage — cooperate or betray. Every prisoner's dilemma can flip the game entirely.

✨ 神职
✨ Deity
预言家Seer＋

每晚查验一名玩家是好人还是狼人。Each night, checks if a player is Good or Evil.

女巫Witch＋

拥有一瓶解药和一瓶毒药。每晚至多使用一瓶，全程不可自救。One antidote and one poison. At most one per night. Cannot save herself.

猎人Hunter＋

出局时可带走一名存活玩家。被毒或梦游不可开枪。When eliminated, may take one surviving player. Cannot fire if poisoned or sleepwalking.

摄梦人Dreamwalker＋

每晚必须选择一名玩家进入梦游，该玩家免疫狼刀与毒药。Forces one player into sleepwalking each night — immune to kills and poison.

👤 村民
👤 Villager
平民 ×4Villager ×4＋

无特殊技能，凭直觉与逻辑找出狼人。No special ability. Use logic and instinct to find the wolves.

🐺 狼人
🐺 Werewolf
狼人 ×3Werewolf ×3＋

每晚共同击杀一名玩家。Each night, collectively eliminate one player.

典狱长Warden＋

首夜与狼人一同行动，全程不可自爆。从第二夜起率先行动，选择两名玩家进入囚笼。囚笼内两人互相确认号码后决定交易或背叛：双方交易则均免于夜间伤害；双方背叛则技能目标互相转移；手势不同则交易者出局。典狱长在囚笼内时，两名囚犯均免疫狼刀和毒药，手势相同则典狱长出局，不同则另一方出局。Acts with wolves on night one; cannot self-destruct. From night two, acts first — chooses two players for the cage. Both reveal numbers and choose cooperate or betray: both cooperate = both immune to night kills; both betray = skill targets swap; different choices = the cooperating player exits. When the Warden is in the cage, both prisoners are immune to kills and poison.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

孤注一掷为12人竞技版型，典狱长每夜制造囚徒困境，以下为完整游戏流程。

A 12-player competitive template. The Warden creates a nightly prisoner's dilemma. Below is the complete game flow.

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共12张：预言家、女巫、猎人、摄梦人各1张，典狱长1张，狼人3张，平民4张。

The host secretly deals 12 role cards: 1 Seer, 1 Witch, 1 Hunter, 1 Dreamwalker, 1 Warden, 3 Werewolves, and 4 Villagers.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

所有人闭眼。狼人（含典狱长）互相睁眼确认身份。女巫查看刀口，预言家查验一名玩家，摄梦人选择梦游目标。典狱长首夜与狼人一同行动，从第二夜起率先选择两名玩家进入囚笼。

All close eyes. Wolves (including the Warden) open to identify each other. Witch sees the kill; Seer checks one player; Dreamwalker selects a target. The Warden acts with wolves on night one; from night two, acts first to select two players for the cage.

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参选玩家投票选出警长。警长投票权重为1.5票，出局时可传递或销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff's vote counts as 1.5 and may pass or destroy the badge upon elimination.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。猎人若被狼刀击杀，可在遗言阶段选择是否开枪带走一名存活玩家。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement. If the Hunter is killed by wolves, they may choose to take a surviving player with them.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

囚笼内两名玩家互相确认号码后同时选择交易或背叛：双方交易则均免于夜间伤害；双方背叛则技能目标互相转移；手势不同则交易者出局。典狱长在囚笼内时规则有所不同，请咨询主持人。

Caged players simultaneously choose cooperate or betray: both cooperate = both immune to night kills; both betray = skill targets swap; different choices = the cooperating player exits. Special rules apply when the Warden is in the cage — ask your host.

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
