Synopsis

静谧的黑夜笼罩在欲欲消人的街道，在惨白的灯光照不到的地方，蠕动的恐影正一点一地逼近。衣着神秘的蜕变俱乐部，一场特殊的私人拍卖晚宴突然降临黑暗中。不可名状的怪物已经来到你们的身边。

身份迥异的老板突然消失，不可名状的怪物正在爬近。随藏在历史阴影中的神秘组织，出没于都市传说聚集的诡异怪物。那场震惊世界的恐怖袭击，那宗会震惊离奇的09凶杀案件，还有近百年前那场惊天动乱令案都从未被披露真实的始末……

如果我说，这还不是最可怕的，你会相信我吗？
如果我说，你并不是你，你会相信我吗？

在未知的恐惧之中，死亡就像一种恩赐。欢迎来到——蜕变俱乐部！

The silent night blankets streets that seem to swallow people whole. In the places where the pallid light cannot reach, creeping shadows draw closer inch by inch. A mysterious private auction soirée descends without warning upon the Transformation Club — shrouded in darkness. Something indescribable has already drawn near.

An enigmatic host vanishes without trace. Nameless things crawl ever closer. A clandestine organisation lurks in the shadow of history; unspeakable creatures haunt the edges of urban legend. A world-shocking act of terror. The bizarre and unsolved murders of '09. Upheavals from a century past, whose true origins have never been made known…

If I told you this was not even the most terrifying part — would you believe me?
If I told you that you are not who you think you are — would you believe me?

In the midst of unknown terror, death feels like a mercy. Welcome — to the Transformation Club.
