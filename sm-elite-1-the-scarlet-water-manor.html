Synopsis

民国三年（公元1914年）8月18日。山西晋城，铁路泽州站东南的角山半腰，巍然矗立着一座豪宅大院——丹水山庄。

大院主人赵万雷耗资巨万，融合传统晋院与西式楼阁，又凿山引泉，建流水荷塘。他亲题对联：「斯夜未央，宇榭笙歌达旦；文明已开，刀楼洋舞通宵。」山庄建成之日，族里老人叹道：富骄奢极，善终难矣。

这一天，一场不幸如预料般降临在山庄之内，几个局内人的生命也从此纠缠在了一起……

August 18th, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). Halfway up Jiao Mountain, southeast of Zezhou Station in Jincheng, Shanxi, stands a magnificent manor — the Scarlet Water Manor.

Its owner, 赵万雷, spared no fortune in its construction, blending traditional Shanxi courtyard architecture with Western pavilions, and even diverted a mountain spring to feed a lotus pond. He inscribed a couplet at the gate: "The night is endless, music fills the manor till dawn; civilisation has arrived, foreign dances last till morning." On the day of its completion, an elder of the clan sighed: "Such extravagance — a good end will be hard to come by."

On this day, misfortune descends upon the manor as fated — and the lives of those within become forever entangled…
