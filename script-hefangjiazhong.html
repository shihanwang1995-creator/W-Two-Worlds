☀️ 镇民☀️ Townsfolk
贵族Noble＋

背景故事
「讽刺的确是最不足挂齿的机智。尽管如此，先生，回应你的批评，就是一种机智。」

角色能力
在你的首个夜晚，你会得知三名玩家：其中有且只有一名玩家是邪恶的。

角色简介
贵族能得知三名玩家中的一名是邪恶的。贵族只会在第一个夜晚得知他的信息。如果贵族在游戏中途被创造，他会在自己作为贵族时的第一个夜晚得知信息。贵族得知的是两名善良玩家和一名邪恶玩家，他不会得知一名善良玩家和两名邪恶玩家，也不会得知三名邪恶玩家。Information
"Sarcasm is indeed the lowest form of wit. But speaking in response to your criticism, Sir, it is, nevertheless, a form of wit."

Summary
"You start knowing 3 players, 1 and only 1 of which is evil."
The Noble learns that one of three players is evil.
· The Noble learns their information on the first night only.
· If a Noble is created mid-game, the Noble learns their information on their first night.
· The Noble learns two good players and one evil player. They may not learn one good player and two evil players. They may not learn three evil players.

小精灵Pixie＋

背景故事
「绕着花园转圈圈，女孩跑得疯癫癫。男孩树上荡秋千，小精灵在谁心间？淑女微笑小镇边，贵族抡斧树长眠。容貌相似难分辨，谜底揭晓圣光现：小精灵呀是神仙。」

角色能力
在你的首个夜晚，你会得知一个在场的镇民角色。如果你「疯狂」地证明你是该角色，当他死亡时你获得该角色的能力。

角色简介
小精灵会假装成与别人相同的角色。在首个夜晚，小精灵会得知一个在场的镇民角色，由说书人来决定他得知的是哪个镇民角色。小精灵不会知道哪名玩家是这个角色。如果小精灵假装他是这个镇民，他会在对应的镇民玩家死亡时获得这个镇民角色的能力。由说书人来判断小精灵是否有尝试在「疯狂」地证明自己是这个角色。当那个镇民玩家死亡时，小精灵不会得知这一信息，也不会被告知自己获得了新的能力。如果小精灵得知的角色所对应的玩家的角色发生了变化，并随后死亡，小精灵会获得他得知的那个角色的能力，而非那名玩家死亡时的角色的能力。Information
"Round and round the garden, go. Little girls run to and fro. Little boys climb up the tree. Which of these should Pixie be? Ladies smile and go to town. Lords with axe chop forest down. What's yours is mine. What's mine, divine. Silly little Pixie, me."

Summary
"You start knowing 1 in-play Townsfolk. If you were mad that you were this character, you gain their ability when they die."
The Pixie pretends to be the same character as someone else.
· On the first night, the Pixie learns an in-play Townsfolk. The Storyteller chooses which Townsfolk this is. The Pixie does not learn which player is this character.
· If the Pixie pretends to be this Townsfolk, they gain the ability of this Townsfolk when the Townsfolk dies. The Storyteller judges whether the player was convincingly "mad that they are this character".
· When the Townsfolk dies, the Pixie is not told that they have gained a new ability. They may learn this if they wake at night and gain information or are prompted to choose players.
· If the player the Pixie learns about changes character then dies, the Pixie gains the ability of the Townsfolk the Pixie learnt about, not the new character.

传教士Preacher＋

每个夜晚，你要选择一名玩家：如果你选中了爪牙，他得知被传教士选中。所有被你选中的爪牙失去能力。Each night, choose a player: a Minion, if chosen, learns this. All chosen Minions have no ability.

气球驾驶员Balloonist＋

背景故事
「再来点热量！高点！高点！再高点！啊~从这上面往下看，风景真美，你说呢？你能看见孩子们在河边柳树下钓鱼吗？你能看到马戏团帐篷杆上反射的阳光吗？这是什么？一位老人孤身一人在葡萄园里昏倒了？少来点热量！低点！低点！再低点！」

角色能力
每个夜晚，你会得知一名与上个夜晚得知的玩家角色类型不同的玩家。[+0~1外来者]

角色简介
气球驾驶员能得知不同角色类型的玩家。气球驾驶员每次必须得知不同于上一次得知玩家的角色类型的玩家。气球驾驶员不会得知玩家的角色类型。向气球驾驶员展示的玩家可以存活或死亡，可以善良或邪恶。如果气球驾驶员中毒或醉酒，他可以得知与上一次得知玩家的角色类型相同的玩家。如果之后气球驾驶员恢复了健康清醒，他们必须得知与上一次得知玩家的角色类型不同的玩家。设置调整阶段，说书人可以决定是否让气球驾驶员添加一名额外的外来者。Information
"More heat! Higher! Higher! Più alto! Ahhh... it is so beautiful from up here, don't you agree? Can you see the children fishing by the river, under the willow? Can you see the glint of the sun on the circus tent-poles? What's this? An old man, alone, passed out in the vineyard? Less heat! Lower! Lower! Vai più in basso!"

Summary
"Each night, you learn a player of a different character type than last night. [+0 or +1 Outsider]"
The Balloonist learns players of different character types.
· Each time the Balloonist learns a player, the player must have a different character type to the previously shown player.
· The Balloonist does not learn the character type of the player they learn.
· The shown player can be alive or dead, and good or evil.
· If the Balloonist is drunk or poisoned, they may learn a character of the same type as the previously shown player. When sober and healthy, they must learn a player of a different character type.
· During setup, the Storyteller may choose to add an Outsider due to the Balloonist's ability.

神谕者Oracle＋

背景故事
「唯有受选者才能凝视面纱之外的光景。亡者躁动不安……他们的每一根僵硬的手指都指向了刺骨的北地。」

角色能力
每个夜晚＊，你会得知有多少名死亡的玩家是邪恶的。

角色简介
神谕者会得知有多少死亡玩家是邪恶的。由于神谕者每晚在恶魔之后行动，所以神谕者的信息以当晚黎明时的状态为基准，他得知的是在所有玩家都睁开眼睛时，死亡的邪恶玩家数量。神谕者能力能检测死去的爪牙和恶魔，以及任何属于邪恶阵营的玩家。在计算死亡玩家的数量时，请记住计算在魔典中倒置的镇民和外来者角色标记，因为这意味着他们现在属于邪恶阵营。Information
"Only the chosen may gaze beyond the veil. The dead are restless, and they point in silence toward the icy north."

Summary
"Each night*, you learn how many dead players are evil."
The Oracle knows how many dead players are evil.
· Because the Oracle acts after the Demon attacks each night, the Oracle's info refers to the players that are dead when dawn breaks and all players open their eyes.
· The Oracle detects dead Minions and Demons, but also any other players that are evil, such as evil Travellers, or Townsfolk and Outsiders that have been turned evil.
· When counting the number of dead players, remember to count Townsfolk and Outsider tokens that are upside-down, which means their alignment is the opposite of what is printed.

国王King＋

每个夜晚，如果死亡的玩家数量大于存活的玩家数量，你会得知一个存活角色。恶魔知道你是国王。Each night, if the dead equal or outnumber the living, you learn 1 alive character. The Demon knows you are the King.

村夫Village Idiot＋

背景故事
「玫瑰花是蓝色哒！草桂花是红色哒！哦豁，俺说反啦！」

角色能力
每个夜晚，你要选择一名玩家：你会得知他的阵营。[+0~2村夫，复数村夫中有一人醉酒]

角色简介
村夫结伴而行，他们能得知玩家的阵营。由说书人在进行游戏设置时决定哪一名村夫醉酒。在场村夫的数量可能是1、2或3，这与一局游戏的玩家人数无关。如果在场村夫的数量为1，他会是清醒的。醉酒的村夫仍然可能会得知正确信息。在游戏设置时如果添加了额外的村夫，他们会替换其他的镇民角色标记。如果村夫在游戏过程中被创造，只有被创造的村夫会发生角色变化。多名村夫在夜晚会分别独自行动，而不是一同被唤醒。如果所有清醒的村夫都离场，剩余的醉酒村夫仍然保持醉酒状态。Information
"Roses are blue, and violets are red, Please reverse what I just said."

Summary
"Each night, choose a player: you learn their alignment. [+0 to +2 Village Idiots. 1 of the extras is drunk]"
The Village Idiots are a group that learn players' alignments.
· The Village Idiot that is drunk is chosen by the Storyteller during the game setup.
· There may be one, two, or three Village Idiots in play, irrespective of the number of players.
· If there is only one Village Idiot in play, they are sober.
· The drunk Village Idiot may get true information.
· When Village Idiots are added to the game during setup, they replace other Townsfolk.
· If a Village Idiot is created mid-game, only one is created.
· Village Idiots act one at a time, not all together.
· If all sober Village Idiots exit play, the remaining drunk Village Idiot remains drunk.

异教领袖Cult Leader＋

每个夜晚，你会转变为与你邻近的一名存活的玩家的阵营。每个白天，你可以提议所有玩家加入你的教派，如果所有善良玩家同意加入，你的阵营获胜。Each night, you become the alignment of an alive neighbor. If all good players choose to join your cult, your team wins.

半兽人Lycanthrope＋

背景故事
「在文明的浅薄外表之下，藏着呼之欲出的疯狂。」

角色能力
每个夜晚＊，你要选择一名存活玩家：如果他是善良的，他死亡，并且当晚恶魔不会造成死亡。会有一名善良玩家始终被当作邪恶阵营。

角色简介
半兽人会在夜晚游荡，猎杀无辜，与此同时恶魔在自己的老巢里蛰伏。半兽人必须在每个夜晚选择一名存活的玩家。如果半兽人选择的玩家是善良玩家，那名玩家死亡，并且当晚恶魔不能造成死亡。如果半兽人选择的玩家是邪恶玩家，那名玩家不会死亡，并且恶魔可以造成死亡。如果半兽人攻击了善良的玩家但没有导致那名玩家死亡，恶魔可以造成死亡。恶魔与造成死亡无关的能力仍然会正常运作。一名善良玩家始终被当作邪恶阵营，半兽人无法杀死这名玩家。Information
"Beneath the thin veneer of civilisation lies a howling madness."

Summary
"Each night*, choose an alive player. If good, they die & the Demon doesn't kill tonight. One good player registers as evil."
The Lycanthrope roams at night, killing the innocent, whilst the Demon cowers indoors.
· The Lycanthrope must choose an alive player each night. If the Lycanthrope chooses a dead player, the Storyteller shakes their head and prompts them to choose a different player.
· If the player the Lycanthrope chooses is good, that player dies, and the Demon cannot kill tonight.
· If the player the Lycanthrope attacks is evil, that player does not die, and the Demon may still kill tonight.
· If the Lycanthrope attacks a good player but that player doesn't die, the Demon may still kill tonight.
· While the Lycanthrope lives, one good player registers as evil. They cannot be killed by the Lycanthrope.

女裁缝Seamstress＋

背景故事
「你听到那个穿羊绒大衣的家伙对我们小贝尔说什么了吗？他说……「好」？哎，行吧，和哈利还有那个杂耍艺人那一出相比，这算不了什么。说什么？我可不说，我又不是什么长舌妇。」

角色能力
每局游戏限一次，在夜晚时，你可以选择除你以外的两名玩家：你会得知他们是否为同一阵营。

角色简介
女裁缝能得知两名玩家是否属于同一阵营。女裁缝每场游戏只能得到一次信息，所以女裁缝最好是选择最佳时机来使用能力，还要选择最关键的玩家。女裁缝可以选择除自己外的任何玩家，无论他是生是死，甚至可以选择旅行者。Information
"Did you hear that stranger in the cashmere coat put the word on our young Belle? And she said yes? Well, that's nothing compared to what Harry and that juggler got up to at the fair! The things I could say if I was a tattletale... my, yes."

Summary
"Once per game, at night, choose 2 players (not yourself): you learn if they are the same alignment."
The Seamstress learns whether 2 players are on the same team as each other.
· They only get this information once per game, so they had best choose wisely when and who.
· They may choose alive or dead players or even Travellers.

博学者Savant＋

背景故事
「地上有七十二根火柴……今天的太阳落得更早，月亮还是按时升起……一块破布……庄园里的邪恶之物……三个又三个……我们相信的事物和他真正看到的不一样……绿光代表镁……残留物，但是图案不太对……地上有七十二根火柴……」

角色能力
每个白天，你可以私下询问说书人以得知两条信息：一个是正确的，一个是错误的。

角色简介
博学者能得知惊人的信息，每一个白天，甚至每一局游戏里他能得到的信息都不会相同，但其中一半是错误的。每个白天，说书人选择两条信息提供给博学者——一条必须是正确的，一条必须是错误的，博学者不知道哪条对哪条错。是否与说书人交谈取决于博学者而非说书人。博学者从说书人处获取信息不是公开交流，博学者会秘密地获取信息。博学者可以选择不从说书人那里获取信息。醉酒或中毒的博学者可能会得到两条正确信息或两条错误信息。Information
"Seventy-two matchsticks on the floor... the sun sets early but the moon is unchanged... a torn piece of cloth... evil in the manor house... three by three... the one we trusted is not what he seems... green light means magnesium... residue, but the pattern is wrong... Seventy-two matchsticks on the floor..."

Summary
"Each day, you may visit the Storyteller to learn 2 things in private: 1 is true & 1 is false."
The Savant gets crazy, amazing information that is different every day and every game, but exactly half of it is completely false.
· Each day, the Storyteller chooses two pieces of information to give the Savant... so get creative! One must be true, and one must be false, and the Savant won't know which is which.
· It is up to the Savant to talk with the Storyteller, not the other way around. This isn't a public conversation, and the group can't listen in. It's private.
· The Savant can choose to not visit the Storyteller if they wish.
· A drunk or poisoned Savant might get two pieces of true information or two pieces of false information.

食人族Cannibal＋

背景故事
「我讨厌小丑的味道。吃起来会让我想笑。」

角色能力
你拥有上个死于处决的玩家的能力。如果该玩家属于邪恶阵营，你中毒直到下个善良玩家死于处决。

角色简介
食人族会吃下被处决的角色，并获得他们的能力。如果一名善良玩家死于处决，食人族能获得他的能力。如果一名邪恶玩家死于处决，食人族也只会认为自己获得了能力，因为他中毒了，说书人可能会对他撒谎。每当有一名玩家死于处决时，食人族会失去上一次获得的能力。处决一名已经死亡的玩家不会让食人族获得能力。处决一名存活但并未导致死亡的玩家也不会让食人族获得能力。玩家必须要被处决并因此死亡，才会让食人族获得能力。食人族不会被告知自己获得了什么能力。如果食人族获得了「即使你已死亡」的能力，例如陌客，或在死亡后才会生效的能力，例如守鸦人或心上人，食人族会在死亡时仍然保留这些能力，但失去食人族自己角色本身的能力。Information
"I don't like clowns. They taste funny."

Summary
"You have the ability of the recently killed executee. If they are evil, you are poisoned until a good player dies by execution."
The Cannibal eats executed characters, gaining their ability.
· If a good player dies by execution, the Cannibal gains that player's ability. If an evil player dies by execution, the Cannibal is poisoned and the Storyteller may be lying to them.
· Each time a player dies by execution, the Cannibal loses the ability of the previous player.
· Executing a dead player won't grant the Cannibal an ability. A player must be executed and die for the Cannibal to gain their ability.
· The Cannibal is not told which ability they have gained. They must figure that out for themselves.
· If the Cannibal has an "even if dead" ability, such as the Recluse, or an ability that implies it works while dead, such as the Ravenkeeper or Sweetheart, the Cannibal keeps that ability when they die, but loses their Cannibal ability.

唱诗男孩Choirboy＋

如果恶魔杀死了国王，你会得知哪名玩家是恶魔。[＋国王]If the Demon kills the King, you learn which player is the Demon. [+the King]

🌫️ 外来者🌫️ Outsider
陌客Recluse＋

背景故事
「侬康康窝咦锅惹，咋锅就怼窝恁锅粗暴。介样叭行。麻绳添酒嘿料，泥滴燕绳恨舔没，窝这哈圆酿泥料！侬晓得伐！窝叭象载康岛泥鸟，辣过饿魔阔能揍载窝家边乱晃。呔！窝揍斯弄个以斯！」

角色能力
你可能会被当作邪恶阵营、爪牙角色或恶魔角色，即使你已死亡。

角色简介
陌客会看起来像是邪恶一员，但实际上是善良的。任何时候要探查陌客的阵营时，由说书人来决定陌客被当作善良还是邪恶。任何时候陌客被影响特定的爪牙或恶魔角色的能力选择时，由说书人来决定陌客是否会被当作某种特定的爪牙或是恶魔角色。陌客能在同一个夜晚的不同能力中分别被当作善良或邪恶阵营、外来者、爪牙或恶魔角色，由说书人来选择最有趣的结果。陌客在被当作特定的爪牙或恶魔角色时，不会获得这一角色的能力。例如，被当作投毒者的陌客无法在夜晚被唤醒，也无法让一名玩家中毒。Information
"Garn git ya darn grub ya mitts ofma lorn yasee. Grr. Natsy pikkins yonder southwise ye begittin afta ya! Git! Me harvy no so widda licks and demmons no be fightin' hadsup ne'er ma kin. Git, assay!"

Summary
"You might register as evil & as a Minion or Demon, even if dead."
The Recluse might appear to be an evil character, but is actually good.
· Whenever the Recluse's alignment is detected, the Storyteller chooses whether the Recluse registers as good or evil.
· Whenever the Recluse is targeted by an ability that affects specific Minions or Demons, the Storyteller chooses whether the Recluse registers as that specific Minion or Demon.
· The Recluse may register as either good or evil, or as an Outsider, Minion, or Demon, at different parts of the same night. The Storyteller chooses whatever is most interesting.
· A Recluse that registers as a particular Minion or Demon does not have this character's ability. For example, a Recluse that registers as a Poisoner does not wake at night and cannot poison a player.

畸形秀演员Mutant＋

背景故事
「我不是怪物！我是人！求你了！」

角色能力
如果你「疯狂」地证明自己是外来者，你可能被处决。

角色简介
畸形秀演员如果试图揭露自己是谁，他就会被处决。「疯狂」是一个术语，意思是「你正在尝试说服他人相信某件事情」。说服的形式包含口头暗示自己的角色，也包含在自己的角色被质疑时的保持沉默。畸形秀演员是否「疯狂」由说书人来决定。如果你认为他正在以任何方式证明自己是一名外来者，你就可以处决他，即使在提名阶段之外，甚至是在晚上都可以处决他。如果你这样做，正常情况下这意味今天白天不会再有其他人被处决。如果畸形秀演员晚上暗示他是畸形秀演员，那么你在当晚可以处决他，即使今天白天已经有人被处决。宣布他的死亡，并正常进行夜晚。明天仍然可能发生一场处决。Information
"I am not a freak! I am a human being! Have mercy!"

Summary
"If you are 'mad' about being an Outsider, you might be executed."
The Mutant is killed if they try to reveal who they are.
· "Madness" is a term that means "you are trying to convince the group of something." If the Mutant player is mad about being an Outsider, this means they are trying to convince people that they are an Outsider.
· This can be by verbally hinting who they are, or by their silence when questioned. It is always up to the Storyteller to decide what the Mutant is doing. If you think they are trying to convince the group they are an Outsider in any way, you can execute them—even outside the nomination phase, or at night.
· If the Mutant hints that they are the Mutant at night, you may execute them that night, even if an execution happened today. Declare they have died, and continue the night phase as normal. An execution may still happen the next day.

角色简介

"When one begins to think that some thing is merely some other thing, one is usually on the brink of an error. Patience, patience. Don't confuse just and should with is and isn't."

Summary
"1 player is drunk, even if you die. If you guess (once) who it is, learn the Demon player, but guess wrong & get false info."
The Puzzlemaster tries to figure out who is drunk.
· A player is drunk for the whole game—usually a Townsfolk, but could be an Outsider. This player does not know they are drunk.
· Once per game, the Puzzlemaster may guess which player it is, publicly or privately. The Storyteller privately tells the Puzzlemaster a player's name. If correct, they learn the Demon. If incorrect, they learn a different player instead.
· The Puzzlemaster isn't told if they guessed correctly or not.
· Only the player made drunk by the Puzzlemaster counts as a successful guess. Players drunk by other means don't count.
· A dead Puzzlemaster may not make a guess, but the drunk player is still drunk.
