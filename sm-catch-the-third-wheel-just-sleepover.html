在基情四射的八星级大酒店汗停宾馆里，一对新人即将步入婚姻的坟墓。新婚前夜，捉J在床，香艳场面闪瞎了所有人的眼。

这究竟是人性的泯灭还是道德的沦丧，谁是真正的"有点东西"……暖气观察持续为您报道！

In the sweltering rooms of the eight-star Hanting Hotel, a couple is about to walk into the grave called marriage. The night before the wedding, an in-flagrante scandal explodes — blinding every eye in the room.

"Catch the mistress! Don't let that J-person get away!"
"We're just sleeping together — it's purely platonic!"
"Honey, say something — I'm NOT the other woman! The one who isn't loved is the other woman!"
"Photos! Take photos! I'm posting this on my story!"

Is this the death of humanity or the collapse of morality? Who is truly "something else"? Nuanqi Observation brings you continuous coverage!
