Synopsis

八个无名无姓之人在一个封闭的建筑内完成了"七人无人生还"的大逃杀诡计，不可能成为凶手的唯一幸存者留下了绝无谎言的记录。每一个密室的背后都被名为疑点的烟雾笼置。当我们了解了这个世界的规则之后，才意识到该如何找到突破口……

火是烟的因、烟是火的果。我们已经对照结果找到了唯一的源头，突如其来的十个悖论却让推理链条无法闭环！

一起谜案、三个世界、六个密室、十个悖论、六十七个结果、一个解答。

Eight nameless individuals complete a deadly "seven must die" survival scheme inside a sealed building. The sole survivor — who could not possibly be the killer — leaves behind a record with no lies. Behind every locked room hangs a fog of doubt. Only once we understand the rules of this world do we realise where the breakthrough must lie…

Fire is the cause of smoke, and smoke is the effect of fire. We've traced the results back to a single source — but ten sudden paradoxes have shattered the chain of reasoning before it can close.

One mystery. Three worlds. Six locked rooms. Ten paradoxes. Sixty-seven possible outcomes. One answer.
