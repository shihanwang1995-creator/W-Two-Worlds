OVERVIEW

模板简介

Overview

游戏开始时，两名随机神职秘密变为狼人阵营，仍单独行动。神职与狼人身份界限模糊，极度混乱。

At game start, two random deity roles secretly become wolves — they still act alone. The line between deity and wolf blurs completely.

✨ 神职✨ Deity
预言家Seer＋

每晚可以查验一名玩家的身份是好人还是狼人。Each night, check whether a player is Good or Evil.

女巫Witch＋

拥有一瓶解药和一瓶毒药，持有解药时可看到狼人当夜刀口。每晚至多使用一瓶药，全程不可自救。Holds one antidote and one poison. When holding the antidote, sees the wolf kill target. At most one potion per night. Cannot save herself.

猎人Hunter＋

出局时可带走一名存活玩家。被毒不可开枪。When eliminated, may take one surviving player. Cannot fire if poisoned.

熊Bear＋

存活状态下，若两边存在狼人阵营玩家，则白天法官宣布熊咆哮。熊死亡后默认不咆哮。While alive, if wolves are on both sides, the host announces a Bear roar during the day. No roar after Bear dies.

魔术师Magician＋

每夜交换任意两名存活玩家的号码牌，每局游戏中每个号码牌仅能被使用一次。Each night, swap any two alive players' seat numbers. Each seat number can only be used once per game.

摄梦人Dreamwalker＋

每晚必须选择一名玩家进入梦游。被梦游的玩家免疫狼刀与毒药。若连续两晚梦游同一名玩家，则该玩家出局。摄梦人在夜间出局时，被梦游的玩家跟随出局。Each night, must choose a player to sleepwalk. That player is immune to wolf kills and poison. Same target two nights in a row = that player exits. If Dreamwalker dies at night, the sleepwalking target also exits.

👥 平民👥 Villager
平民Villager＋

无特殊技能，凭直觉与逻辑找出狼人。No special ability. Use logic and instinct to identify the wolves.

🐺 狼人阵营🐺 Wolf Team
狼人Werewolf＋

每晚可以共同击杀一名玩家。白天可以自爆，pk发言除外。Each night, collectively eliminate one player. May self-destruct during the day (not during duel/last words).

狼王Wolf King＋

出局时可带走一名存活玩家。被毒或梦游不可开枪。When eliminated, may take one surviving player. Cannot fire if poisoned or sleepwalking.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共12张：预言家 · 女巫 · 猎人 · 熊 · 魔术师 · 摄梦人 · 狼王 · 狼人 · 平民×4。

The host secretly deals 12 role cards: Seer · Witch · Hunter · Bear · Magician · Dreamwalker · Wolf King · Werewolf · Villager×4.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

所有人闭眼。游戏开始时，两名随机神职秘密变为狼人阵营，仍单独行动，胜利条件与狼人相同。若熊变为狼人则技能同狼美人。各神职按正常流程夜间行动（变为狼人的神职技能失效）。

All close eyes. Two random deity roles secretly become wolves at game start — they act alone and win with the wolf team. If Bear becomes a wolf, their ability functions like Wolf Beauty. All deities act normally at night (wolf-aligned deities' skills are nullified).

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参与竞选的玩家投票选出警长。警长发言顺序享有优先权，投票时拥有1.5票权重。警长出局时可将警徽传递给任意存活玩家，或选择销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff gains priority in speaking order and their vote counts as 1.5. Upon elimination, the Sheriff may pass the badge to any surviving player — or destroy it.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

所有存活玩家轮流发言并投票。由于神职可能是狼人，所有信息均需谨慎判断。狼王出局时可带走一名存活玩家（被毒或梦游不可开枪）。

All surviving players speak and vote. Since any deity may be a wolf, all information must be assessed carefully. Wolf King drags a player on exit (cannot fire if poisoned or sleepwalking).

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
