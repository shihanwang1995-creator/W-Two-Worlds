OVERVIEW · 简介
OVERVIEW

模板简介

Overview

纯白之女查验为狼人时将其当夜击杀，猎魔人每晚狩猎，双重主动击杀让狼队压力极大。

Pure White Girl kills any wolf she checks; Demon Hunter hunts nightly. Dual active-kill mechanics put the wolf team under enormous pressure.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共12张：纯白之女 · 女巫 · 猎魔人 · 大天使 · 觉醒狼王 · 狼人×3 · 平民×4。

The host secretly deals 12 role cards: Pure White · Witch · Demon Hunter · Archangel · Awakened Wolf King · Werewolf×3 · Villager×4.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

所有人闭眼。觉醒狼王与狼人睁眼互认，商定首刀目标。女巫查看刀口。纯白之女查验一名玩家：从第二夜起，若查验到狼人则当夜其出局。大天使选择一名玩家免疫所有负面状态（不可连续两晚选同一人）。从第二夜起，猎魔人选择一名玩家狩猎。

All close eyes. Awakened Wolf King and wolves open and select a kill. Witch sees the kill. Pure White checks one player — from night two, if Evil, they exit that night. Archangel grants one player immunity (cannot choose the same player two nights in a row). From night two, Demon Hunter selects a player to hunt.

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参与竞选的玩家投票选出警长。警长发言顺序享有优先权，投票时拥有1.5票权重。警长出局时可将警徽传递给任意存活玩家，或选择销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff gains priority in speaking order and their vote counts as 1.5. Upon elimination, the Sheriff may pass the badge to any surviving player — or destroy it.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

觉醒狼王拥有两把枪，可在夜间将任意数量的枪传递给存活队友。每把枪每局只能传递一次，带枪狼人自爆时不可开枪。大天使不可连续两晚选同一人。

Awakened Wolf King has two guns and may pass any number of guns to alive teammates at night. Each gun can only be passed once per game; wolves carrying guns cannot fire when self-destructing.

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
