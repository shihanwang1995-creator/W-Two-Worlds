☀️ 镇民☀️ Townsfolk
小精灵Pixie＋

背景故事
「绕着花园转圈圈，女孩跑得疯癫癫。男孩树上荡秋千，小精灵在谁心间？淑女微笑小镇边，贵族抡斧树长眠。容貌相似难分辨，谜底揭晓圣光现：小精灵呀是神仙。」

角色能力
在你的首个夜晚，你会得知一个在场的镇民角色。如果你「疯狂」地证明你是该角色，当他死亡时你获得该角色的能力。

角色简介
小精灵会假装成与别人相同的角色。在首个夜晚，小精灵会得知一个在场的镇民角色，由说书人来决定他得知的是哪个镇民角色。小精灵不会知道哪名玩家是这个角色。如果小精灵假装他是这个镇民，他会在对应的镇民玩家死亡时获得这个镇民角色的能力。由说书人来判断小精灵是否有尝试在「疯狂」地证明自己是这个角色。当那个镇民玩家死亡时，小精灵不会得知这一信息，也不会被告知自己获得了新的能力。如果小精灵得知的角色所对应的玩家的角色发生了变化，并随后死亡，小精灵会获得他得知的那个角色的能力，而非那名玩家死亡时的角色的能力。Information
"Round and round the garden, go. Little girls run to and fro. Little boys climb up the tree. Which of these should Pixie be? Ladies smile and go to town. Lords with axe chop forest down. What's yours is mine. What's mine, divine. Silly little Pixie, me."

Summary
"You start knowing 1 in-play Townsfolk. If you were mad that you were this character, you gain their ability when they die."
The Pixie pretends to be the same character as someone else.
· On the first night, the Pixie learns an in-play Townsfolk. The Storyteller chooses which Townsfolk this is. The Pixie does not learn which player is this character.
· If the Pixie pretends to be this Townsfolk, they gain the ability of this Townsfolk when the Townsfolk dies. The Storyteller judges whether the player was convincingly "mad that they are this character".
· When the Townsfolk dies, the Pixie is not told that they have gained a new ability. They may learn this if they wake at night and gain information or are prompted to choose players.
· If the player the Pixie learns about changes character then dies, the Pixie gains the ability of the Townsfolk the Pixie learnt about, not the new character.

贵族Noble＋

背景故事
「讽刺的确是最不足挂齿的机智。尽管如此，先生，回应你的批评，就是一种机智。」

角色能力
在你的首个夜晚，你会得知三名玩家：其中有且只有一名玩家是邪恶的。

角色简介
贵族能得知三名玩家中的一名是邪恶的。贵族只会在第一个夜晚得知他的信息。如果贵族在游戏中途被创造，他会在自己作为贵族时的第一个夜晚得知信息。贵族得知的是两名善良玩家和一名邪恶玩家，他不会得知一名善良玩家和两名邪恶玩家，也不会得知三名邪恶玩家。Information
"Sarcasm is indeed the lowest form of wit. But speaking in response to your criticism, Sir, it is, nevertheless, a form of wit."

Summary
"You start knowing 3 players, 1 and only 1 of which is evil."
The Noble learns that one of three players is evil.
· The Noble learns their information on the first night only.
· If a Noble is created mid-game, the Noble learns their information on their first night.
· The Noble learns two good players and one evil player. They may not learn one good player and two evil players. They may not learn three evil players.

修行者Shugenja＋

在你的首个夜晚，你会得知距离最近的邪恶玩家位于你的顺时针还是逆时针方向。如果两侧的邪恶玩家与你距离相等，你得知的信息由说书人决定。You start knowing if your closest evil player is clockwise or anti-clockwise. If equidistant, this info is arbitrary.

筑梦师Dreamer＋

背景故事
「我记得有钟表匠……天空是红色的，有不规则的三角形不断从天上坠落下来。有紫罗兰的气味……还有气泡咕嘟咕嘟的声音。一个眼睛发光、还有着乱糟糟的胡须的女人对着天空发出嘶嘶声。然后，我醒了……」

角色能力
每个夜晚，你要选择除你及旅行者以外的一名玩家：你会得知一个善良角色和一个邪恶角色，该玩家是其中一个角色。

角色简介
筑梦师能得知一名玩家可能对应的角色，但不能确认哪个是正确的。每个夜晚，筑梦师需要选择一名其他玩家，并得知一个善良角色与一个邪恶角色，其中一个是那名玩家的真实角色。筑梦师得知的错误角色标记取决于所选玩家的真正的角色类型。如果筑梦师选择的玩家是镇民或外来者，对应的错误的邪恶角色可以是爪牙或恶魔。如果筑梦师选择的玩家是爪牙或恶魔，对应的错误的善良角色可以是镇民或外来者。筑梦师不能选择自己和旅行者作为目标。Information
"I remember the Clockmaker. The sky was red and it was raining fractal triangles. There was a smell of violets and a bubbling sound. A woman with glowing eyes and a scraggly beard was hissing at the sky. Then, I awoke."

Summary
"Each night, choose a player (not yourself or Travellers): you learn 1 good & 1 evil character, 1 of which is correct."
The Dreamer learns players' characters, but is not sure if their information is entirely correct.
· Each night, the Dreamer chooses a player and learns two characters—one that the player is, and one that the player isn't.
· The false character token depends on the chosen player's true character type. If the Dreamer chooses a Townsfolk or Outsider, the false character is any Minion or Demon. If they choose a Minion or Demon, the false character is a Townsfolk or Outsider.
· The Dreamer may not choose themself, and may not choose a Traveller.

女祭司High Priestess＋

背景故事
「在人性的背后，存在着以人性为面具的生活。某些时候生活会摘下面具，以深刻的答案应对深刻的问题。」

角色能力
每个夜晚，你会得知一名说书人认为你最应该与其交流的玩家。

角色简介
女祭司凭借直觉行事。女祭司可以连续几个夜晚得知同一名玩家，也可以在每个夜晚都得知不同的玩家。她得知的玩家可以是存活或死亡，善良或邪恶。没有绝对的标准用于决定说书人究竟要给女祭司什么样的信息。说书人通过自己的判断来决定给出女祭司的信息内容，这取决于他认为给出什么样的信息能够对女祭司和善良阵营更加有利。这可能是因为某些玩家有重要信息但尚未公布，或是邪恶玩家的伪装一戳即破，或是有值得被信赖的玩家需要建立更强的信任，或是有善良玩家的推理出现了谬误急需被纠正，或是其他各种各样的原因。Information
"There is life behind the personality that uses personalities as masks. There are times when life puts off the mask and deep answers to deep."

Summary
"Each night, learn which player the Storyteller believes you should talk to most."
The High Priestess acts on intuition.
· The High Priestess can be shown the same player multiple times in a row, or a different player every night.
· The shown player can be alive or dead, and good or evil.
· There are no official criteria that determine which player the Storyteller must show. It is up to the Storyteller's judgement as to what they think will most benefit the High Priestess and the good team. It could be because a player has important unrevealed information, an evil player's bluff doesn't add up, a trustworthy player needs to be trusted more, a good player is on the wrong track, or something else entirely.

将军General＋

每个夜晚，你会得知说书人认为哪个阵营当前更有优势。（善良／邪恶／均势）Each night, you learn which alignment the Storyteller believes is winning: good, evil, or neither.

女裁缝Seamstress＋

背景故事
「你听到那个穿羊绒大衣的家伙对我们小贝尔说什么了吗？他说……「好」？哎，行吧，和哈利还有那个杂耍艺人那一出相比，这算不了什么。说什么？我可不说，我又不是什么长舌妇。」

角色能力
每局游戏限一次，在夜晚时，你可以选择除你以外的两名玩家：你会得知他们是否为同一阵营。

角色简介
女裁缝能得知两名玩家是否属于同一阵营。女裁缝每场游戏只能得到一次信息，所以女裁缝最好是选择最佳时机来使用能力，还要选择最关键的玩家。女裁缝可以选择除自己外的任何玩家，无论他是生是死，甚至可以选择旅行者。Information
"Did you hear that stranger in the cashmere coat put the word on our young Belle? And she said yes? Well, that's nothing compared to what Harry and that juggler got up to at the fair! The things I could say if I was a tattletale... my, yes."

Summary
"Once per game, at night, choose 2 players (not yourself): you learn if they are the same alignment."
The Seamstress learns whether 2 players are on the same team as each other.
· They only get this information once per game, so they had best choose wisely when and who.
· They may choose alive or dead players or even Travellers.

哲学家Philosopher＋

背景故事
「如果你要说什么事情是最真实的？我想是啤酒。痛快喝吧，说不定我们明天就要死了。」

角色能力
每局游戏限一次，在夜晚时，你可以选择一个善良角色：你获得该角色的能力。如果这个角色在场，他醉酒。

角色简介
哲学家能够决定自己的能力是什么。他可以选择获得其他角色的能力，但每局仅限一次。当他这么做时，他获得那个角色的能力，但不会变成那个角色。如果哲学家选择了一个已经在游戏中的角色，该角色对应的玩家醉酒。如果哲学家死亡、醉酒或中毒了，那么那名醉酒的玩家会再次恢复清醒。如果哲学家之前选择了不在场的角色，但之后被选择的角色在场了，被选择的角色对应的玩家也会醉酒。如果哲学家获得了一种在夜晚使用的能力，他将在该角色应该被唤醒的时候被唤醒。如果这个角色能力是「首个夜晚」能力，他会在当晚使用该能力。如果哲学家获取的能力在死亡时生效，例如呆瓜的能力，那么当哲学家死亡后，他选择的角色能力就会生效。Information
"If anything is real, beer is real. Drink, for tomorrow we may die."

Summary
"Once per game, at night, choose a good character: gain that ability. If this character is in play, they are drunk."
The Philosopher has no ability until they decide which character they want to emulate.
· They can do this once per game. When they do so, they gain that character's ability. They do not become that character.
· If the Philosopher chooses a character that is already in play, the player of that character becomes drunk. If the Philosopher then dies or becomes drunk or poisoned, the player they are making drunk becomes sober again.
· If the Philosopher chose a character that was not in play at the time but is in play now, that character is drunk.
· If the Philosopher gains an ability that works at night, they wake when that character would wake. If this ability is used on the first night only, they use it tonight.
· If the Philosopher's ability works while dead, such as the Klutz's, it works if the Philosopher is dead.

守夜人Nightwatchman＋

每局游戏限一次，在夜晚时，你可以选择一名玩家：他会得知你是守夜人。Once per game, at night, choose a player: they learn you are the Nightwatchman.

戏法师Alsaahir＋

每个白天，你可以公开进行一次谁是爪牙，谁是恶魔的猜测。如果你猜对，善良阵营获胜。Each day, if you publicly guess which players are Minion(s) and which are Demon(s), good wins.

渔夫Fisherman＋

背景故事
「这是这条河流里我最喜欢的一截儿……看看阳光是如何让一道彩虹横跨修道院和集市的？这是能让鱼儿长得肥美的最好的地方。我一天天变老，它们也一天天长大。」

角色能力
每局游戏限一次，在白天时，你可以让说书人给你一些能帮助你的阵营获胜的建议。

角色简介
渔夫知道其他人都无从得知的事情：该怎么做。渔夫玩家选择何时使用他的能力。当他拜访说书人时，说书人选择给渔夫什么样的建议。说书人给出的建议不一定是「事实」，而是说书人认为如果被采纳的话，能帮助渔夫获胜的战略性建议。如果渔夫醉酒或中毒，说书人可能会给渔夫不好的建议。Information
"This was my favourite part of the river... see how the sunlight makes a rainbow from the monastery to the market? This was the best place for big fish. And the older I get, the bigger they were."

Summary
"Once per game, during the day, visit the Storyteller for some advice to help your team win."
The Fisherman knows something that nobody else can know: what should be done.
· The Fisherman player chooses when to use their ability.
· When they visit the Storyteller, the Storyteller chooses what piece of advice to give the Fisherman.
· The Storyteller's pieces of advice are not necessarily "facts". They are strategy tips that the Storyteller believes will help the Fisherman win, if they are followed.
· If the Fisherman is drunk or poisoned, the Storyteller may give the Fisherman bad advice.

博学者Savant＋

背景故事
「地上有七十二根火柴……今天的太阳落得更早，月亮还是按时升起……一块破布……庄园里的邪恶之物……三个又三个……我们相信的事物和他真正看到的不一样……绿光代表镁……残留物，但是图案不太对……地上有七十二根火柴……」

角色能力
每个白天，你可以私下询问说书人以得知两条信息：一个是正确的，一个是错误的。

角色简介
博学者能得知惊人的信息，每一个白天，甚至每一局游戏里他能得到的信息都不会相同，但其中一半是错误的。每个白天，说书人选择两条信息提供给博学者——一条必须是正确的，一条必须是错误的，博学者不知道哪条对哪条错。是否与说书人交谈取决于博学者而非说书人。博学者从说书人处获取信息不是公开交流，博学者会秘密地获取信息。博学者可以选择不从说书人那里获取信息。醉酒或中毒的博学者可能会得到两条正确信息或两条错误信息。Information
"Seventy-two matchsticks on the floor... the sun sets early but the moon is unchanged... a torn piece of cloth... evil in the manor house... three by three... the one we trusted is not what he seems... green light means magnesium... residue, but the pattern is wrong... Seventy-two matchsticks on the floor..."

Summary
"Each day, you may visit the Storyteller to learn 2 things in private: 1 is true & 1 is false."
The Savant gets crazy, amazing information that is different every day and every game, but exactly half of it is completely false.
· Each day, the Storyteller chooses two pieces of information to give the Savant... so get creative! One must be true, and one must be false, and the Savant won't know which is which.
· It is up to the Savant to talk with the Storyteller, not the other way around. This isn't a public conversation, and the group can't listen in. It's private.
· The Savant can choose to not visit the Storyteller if they wish.
· A drunk or poisoned Savant might get two pieces of true information or two pieces of false information.

杂耍艺人Juggler＋

背景故事
「下一场表演，我需要一朵花、一袋豆子、一条玩具蛇、一支画笔、和一个电动的树篱修剪机。不过我得和你说，宝贝，这可能是我最后的表演了哦。」

角色能力
在你的首个白天，你可以公开猜测任意玩家的角色最多五次。在当晚，你会得知猜测正确的角色数量。

角色简介
杂耍艺人会冒着风险说服别人在第一天就透露他们的角色，希望尽可能多地猜测说实话的人。在第一个白天，杂耍艺人可以对任意玩家的角色进行猜测。当晚，他会得知他猜对的次数。他必须公开猜测，以使所有玩家听到他猜测的内容。他可以猜测零个角色，或者最多猜测五个角色，他所猜测的玩家和角色可以相同也可以不同。如果杂耍艺人在醉酒或中毒时做出了猜测，但当晚他的能力触发时却清醒且健康，那么说书人仍然会给他真实的信息。Information
"For my next trick, as per request, I will need a flower, a bag of beans, a toy snake, a paintbrush, and a motorized gasoline-powered hedge trimming device. I warn you, this trick may be my last. Oh dear."

Summary
"On your 1st day, publicly guess up to 5 players' characters. That night, you learn how many you got correct."
The Juggler takes the risk of convincing people to reveal their characters on the 1st day, in the hope of guessing as many as possible that are telling the truth.
· On the first day, they may guess which players are which characters. That night, the Juggler learns how many guesses they got right...if they are not killed beforehand.
· They must make their guesses publicly, so everyone hears what is guessed. They may guess zero characters, or up to five characters, and these characters and players may be different or the same.
· If the Juggler made their guesses while drunk or poisoned, but is sober and healthy when their ability triggers that night, then the Storyteller still gives them true information.

🌫️ 外来者🌫️ Outsider
政客Politician＋

如果你是对你的阵营落败负最大责任的人，你转变阵营并获胜，即使你已死亡。If you were the player most responsible for your team losing, you change alignment and win, even if dead.

解谜大师Puzzlemaster＋

一名玩家酒，即使你已死亡。每局游戏限一次，你可以猜测谁是那个酒的玩家，如果你猜对了，你会得知谁是恶魔，但如果你猜错了，你会得知错误的"谁是恶魔"信息。1 player is drunk, even if you die. If you guess (once) who it is, learn the Demon player, but guess wrong and get false info.

食人魔Ogre＋

在你的首个夜晚，你要选择除你以外的一名玩家：你转变为他的阵营，即使你已酒或中毒，但你不知道你转变后的阵营。On your 1st night, choose a player (not yourself): you become their alignment (you don't know which) even if drunk or poisoned.

告密者Snitch＋

在你的首个夜晚，所有爪牙分别会得知三个不在场的角色。Each Minion gets 3 bluffs.

🩸 爪牙🩸 Minion
间谍Spy＋

背景故事
「每个调酒大师心中明白，配方没有麻烦来得更快，双面间谍是麻烦的主菜。」

角色能力
每个夜晚，你能查看魔典。你可能会被当作善良阵营、镇民角色或外来者角色，即使你已死亡。

角色简介
间谍知道所有人的具体角色。间谍看起来像是善良一员，但实际上是邪恶的。如果任何角色的能力会探查或影响善良玩家，间谍可能会被那名角色当作善良阵营。如果任何角色的能力会探查或影响镇民角色或外来者角色，间谍可能会被那名角色当作是一个特定的镇民或外来者角色。由说书人来决定间谍被当作什么，在同一个夜晚间谍能被多次当作不同阵营或不同的角色。间谍在被当作特定的镇民或外来者角色时，不会获得这一角色的能力。例如，被当作猎手的间谍无法猎杀恶魔。Information
"Any brewmaster worth their liquor, knows no concoction pours trouble quicker, than one where spies seem double."

Summary
"Each night, you see the Grimoire. You might register as good & as a Townsfolk or Outsider, even if dead."
The Spy might appear to be a good character, but is actually evil. They also see the Grimoire, so they know the characters (and status) of all players.
· If any character has an ability that would detect or affect a good player, then the Spy might register as good to that character. If any character has an ability that detects Townsfolk or Outsiders, then the Spy might register as a specific Townsfolk or Outsider to that player. It is the Storyteller's choice as to what the Spy registers as, even as many characters or both alignments during the same night.
· A Spy that registers as a particular Townsfolk or Outsider does not have this character's ability. For example, a Spy that registers as a Slayer cannot slay the Demon.

限Xaan＋

在等同于初始外来者数量的夜晚，所有镇民玩家中毒直到下个黄昏。[外来者数量注意]On night X, all Townsfolk are poisoned until dusk. [X Outsiders]

提线木偶Marionette＋

你以为你是一个善良角色，但其实你不是。恶魔会知道你是提线木偶。[提线木偶会与恶魔邻座]You think you are a good character, but you are not. The Demon knows who you are. [You neighbor the Demon]

科学怪人Boffin＋

恶魔拥有一个不在场的善良角色的能力，即使他酒或中毒。你和他都知道是哪个角色。The Demon (even if drunk or poisoned) has a not-in-play good character's ability. You both know which.
