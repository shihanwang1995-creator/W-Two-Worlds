Synopsis

民国三年（公元1914年）8月3日农历六月十二，立秋前五天。上海城外西北的远郊，有一个百里村，村外山上有一座被人遗忘的老宅。十多年前，这里曾是许多人流连忘返之地，夜夜歌舞，车马不绝。老宅主人多金好客，酷爱收集精美的法蓝制品，因此得名"法蓝庄"。

没想到一场无妄之灾悄然降临，"法蓝庄"在一夜之间变得再也无人踏足，一直荒废至今……记忆不一定真实，情感会让人对眼前的真相视而不见。或许，你我都是记忆的傀儡。

The 12th day of the 6th lunar month, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914) — five days before the Start of Autumn. In the distant northwest outskirts of Shanghai lies Baili Village, and on the hillside beyond it stands a forgotten old manor. More than a decade ago, this was a place of endless revelry — music, dance, and a constant stream of guests. Its wealthy and hospitable owner had a particular passion for collecting fine cloisonné ware, and so the estate became known as "Falán Manor."

Then, without warning, calamity struck — and overnight, the manor became a place no one would ever set foot in again. It has lain abandoned ever since… Memory is not always truth. Emotion blinds us to the reality before our eyes. Perhaps we are all, in the end, puppets of memory.
