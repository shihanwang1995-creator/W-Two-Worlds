剧情简介

Synopsis

一场荒诞血腥的审判，让六个背负命运的小人物，被迫关押到一座孤岛之上，而等待他们的是一场更加怪诞的游戏。

冰冷的监管者、残酷的执法者、非人的参赛者，共聚这场生存游戏。

谁死谁生？是死而复生的你，是没有足迹的我，还是无形无影的他？

日落月升，门扉关闭——每当密室的大门开启之时，岛上便会多一具尸体。

海风呼啸、海浪滔滔，你听，这孤岛之上正凑响着一曲悲鸣之歌。

An absurd and bloody trial condemns six ill-fated souls to a desolate island — where an even stranger game awaits them.

A cold-blooded warden, a ruthless enforcer, and players who are less than human converge for this survival game.

Who lives? Who dies? The one who returns from death, the one who leaves no footprints, or the one who casts no shadow?

The sun falls, the moon rises, the doors swing shut — and each time a locked room opens, another body is found on the island.

The sea wind howls, the waves crash endlessly. Listen — a song of screams echoes across this lonely island.
