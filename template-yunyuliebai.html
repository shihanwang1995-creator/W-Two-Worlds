Overview

经典四神配置，神民平衡稳健。白痴翻牌机制带来额外变数，适合追求纯粹推理博弈的玩家。

Classic four-deity balance. The Fool's flip mechanic adds a crucial twist — ideal for pure deduction seekers.

HOW TO PLAY · 游戏流程
HOW TO PLAY

游戏流程

Game Flow

预女猎白为12人标准竞技版型，以下为完整游戏流程。

A 12-player competitive template. Below is the complete game flow.

01
分配身份
Role Assignment

主持人秘密发放身份牌。本局共12张：预言家、女巫、猎人、白痴各1张，狼人4张，平民4张。

The host secretly deals 12 role cards: 1 Seer, 1 Witch, 1 Hunter, 1 Idiot, 4 Werewolves, and 4 Villagers.

02
第一夜行动
Night One

所有人闭眼。狼人睁眼互认队友，商定首刀目标。女巫睁眼查看刀口并决定是否使用解药。预言家睁眼查验一名玩家身份。

All close eyes. Wolves open to identify teammates and select a kill target. Witch opens to see the target and decide whether to save. Seer opens to check one player's identity.

03
警长竞选
Sheriff Election

天亮前进行警长竞选。玩家自愿参选并发言拉票，未参选玩家投票选出警长。警长投票权重为1.5票，出局时可传递或销毁警徽。

Before dawn, players campaign for Sheriff. Non-candidates vote. The Sheriff's vote counts as 1.5 and may pass or destroy the badge upon elimination.

04
天亮公布
Dawn Announcement

主持人公布夜间死亡结果。死亡玩家可留下遗言。猎人若被狼刀击杀，可在遗言阶段选择是否开枪带走一名存活玩家。

The host announces the night's deaths. Deceased may leave a final statement. If the Hunter is killed by wolves, they may choose to take a surviving player with them.

05
白天讨论与投票
Day Discussion & Vote

所有存活玩家轮流发言，分享情报或制造谎言。讨论结束后全体投票，得票最多的玩家被淘汰出局。白痴若被放逐可翻牌保留0.5票权，仍视为存活神职。

All surviving players speak in turn — sharing clues or spinning lies. All then vote; the most-voted player is eliminated. If the Idiot is exiled, they may flip their card and retain 0.5 votes, still counted as a living deity.

06
循环与胜利条件
Loop & Victory

投票结束后进入下一个黑夜，如此循环。好人阵营淘汰所有狼人即获胜；狼人阵营淘汰所有村民或所有神职即获胜。

After each vote, night falls again. The cycle continues. Good wins by eliminating all wolves. Wolves win by eliminating all villagers or all deity roles.
