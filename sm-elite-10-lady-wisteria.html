民国三年（公元1914年）9月4日（农历七月十五）。广东省，"广三铁路"终点三水河口站，渡过西江西行，隐匿在山林间的"宝殊镇"。

全镇人口集中在镇中心，其中以"葛家"家业最大——家主葛继先做过镇里的"保长"，膝下二女：大女儿葛月曼远嫁天津，小女儿葛月萝招了外乡人朱陵山做上门女婿。

三个月前，葛继先被土匪绑走，只找回被折磨得惨不忍睹的尸首。葛月萝悲痛欲绝，迁怒赶走全宅奴仆。殊不知，这只是悲剧的开端……

September 4th, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). The Ghost Festival. Guangdong Province — beyond the terminal of the Guang-San Railway at Sanshui Hekou Station, across the West River, lies the secluded mountain town of Baochu.

The town's most prominent family is the Ge clan. The patriarch 葛继先 once served as the town's headman. His elder daughter married into a family in Tianjin; his younger daughter 葛月萝 took in a son-in-law from outside the town.

Three months ago, 葛继先 was kidnapped by bandits — only his brutally disfigured remains were recovered. 葛月萝, consumed by grief and rage, dismissed every last servant from the household. But this, it seems, was only the beginning of the tragedy…
