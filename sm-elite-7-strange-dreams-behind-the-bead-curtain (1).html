Synopsis

民国三年（公元1914年）8月21日。时值清宣统皇帝逊位后，中华改国体为共和，仿佛曙光初现，同时又逢西方诸国纷争，战火蔓延欧陆，无暇东顾，也给多难中华一次恢复国运的天赐良机。

这一年，民国政府实行省、道、县官制，分一省为数道，更于苏、常之地设苏常道，治所就在苏州——这座比邻太湖、长江的千古名城。说起苏州，就不能不提被誉为"江南园林甲天下，苏州园林甲江南"的各种私家园林——所谓藏山水美景于园内，无论官绅商贾，都对此情有独钟。

"孟园"就是这一百七十多处园林中的一座——这本是前清孟举人告老归乡的居所，世代相传，如今成了苏州"法朗商行"老板陈轻梧的私人别墅——每到月末，陈轻梧都会回到"孟园"休息数日，和妻子小聚，最近因为身体欠佳，这位在商海纵横十多年的前清秀才，第一次有了挑选继承人的念头……

民国三年（1914年）8月21日，苏州"孟园"内，本该畅快游园的几个人却各怀心事，仿佛预感到即将看到的悲剧……

August 21st, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914). Following the abdication of the Qing Emperor Xuantong, China had transformed into a republic — a dawn of sorts. Meanwhile, the great powers of the West were consumed by war spreading across Europe, leaving China momentarily free from foreign pressure, presenting a rare opportunity to restore the nation's fortunes.

That same year, the Republican government reorganised the country into provinces, circuits, and counties. The Sucháng Circuit was established across Suzhou and Changzhou, with Suzhou as its seat — an ancient city nestled between Taihu Lake and the Yangtze River. Suzhou is inseparable from its legendary private gardens, said to be the finest in all of Jiangnan. Officials, merchants, and scholars alike were drawn to these sanctuaries where mountains and water were hidden within garden walls.

"Meng Garden" was one among those hundred and seventy-odd gardens. Once the retirement home of a Qing dynasty scholar named Meng, it had passed through generations until it became the private retreat of 陈轻梧, proprietor of the "Falong Trading Company" in Suzhou. Every month's end, 陈轻梧 would return to Meng Garden to rest for a few days and spend time with his wife. Recently, his health had begun to falter — and for the first time, this former Qing scholar who had navigated the commercial world for over a decade began to think about choosing an heir…

On August 21st, 1914, inside Suzhou's Meng Garden, a few guests had gathered for what should have been a leisurely tour of the grounds — yet each carried a secret weight, as though sensing the tragedy that was about to unfold…
