Synopsis

1914年（民国三年）10月19日。

在河南商丘东南的夏邑附近，有一座距离县城稍远的"祖家庄"，不知从何时开始，"祖家庄"成了被诅咒的"诅家庄"，数年来亡者不断……

庄主祖吴亲口说出"诅家庄"之事后不久，在去年（1913年）突然去世，他的长子传文在今年10月18日纳妾时，不幸遇害……

次日（19日），一位红衣女子在"自出楼"北侧上吊身亡，已死的传文竟然现身在尸体面前，向众人厉声问寻真相……

October 19th, Year 3 of the Republic of China (1914).

Near Xiayi, southeast of Shangqiu in Henan Province, stands a manor known as the "Zu Family Estate." At some point lost to memory, it became cursed — renamed the "Cursed Estate" — and for years, deaths have never ceased…

Not long after the estate's master, Zu Wu, spoke openly of the curse, he died suddenly the previous year (1913). His eldest son, Chuanwen, met a violent end on October 18th of this year, the very night he was taking a concubine…

The following morning (the 19th), a woman in red is found hanged on the north side of the "Zichu Tower." And then the impossible: the already-dead Chuanwen appears before the crowd, demanding in fury that they uncover the truth…
