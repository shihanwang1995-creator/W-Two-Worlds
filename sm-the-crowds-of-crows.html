Synopsis

龙城，依山而建，人杰地灵。相传此地千余年前，便是真龙气运汇聚之地。时光斗转，日月不复，近些年来龙城乱象频发。

这些无法被科学定义的乱象，最终被定义为"灾祸"——此间枉者，皆为"邪崇"。能人异士聚于龙城，浩浩荡荡斩除邪崇——此间行者，则为"乌合"。

他们被称之为"乌合"，即为"乌合"便各怀鬼胎。此一时千古龙城群龙无首，然目之所及之处，皆为——乌合之众。

Longcheng — a city built into the mountainside, blessed with outstanding people and fertile land. Legend holds that over a thousand years ago, this was the place where the fortune of the true dragon converged. Time has turned, the sun and moon no longer shine as they did, and in recent years, chaos has erupted across Longcheng.

These disturbances, impossible to define by science, were ultimately named "calamities" — and those who cause them are called "demon-afflicted." Capable warriors and extraordinary individuals flocked to Longcheng to vanquish the demons — these wayfarers are called the "Crows."

They are called "Crows" — and being a Crow means harbouring your own agenda. In this era of leaderless Longcheng, wherever you look, all you see are the Crowds of Crows.
